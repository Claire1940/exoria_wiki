## Exoria Roblox Guild Guide: Setting Up Your Faction

Starting a guild in Exoria is a significant investment that requires both wealth and reputation. Unlike many other Roblox RPGs where guilds are purely social, Exoria treats them as political entities. To begin, you must locate the High Overseer NPC found in the Capital City's central plaza. In 2026, the requirements have been adjusted to ensure that only dedicated players can lead a faction.

Before approaching the High Overseer, ensure you meet the minimum level and currency requirements. Guilds are permanent fixtures unless disbanded, so choosing a name and tag is a critical first step that cannot be easily reversed without premium currency.

### Guild Creation Requirements

| Requirement Type | Minimum Threshold | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Player Level** | Level 45+ | Required for the Guild Master role. |
| **Currency (Gold)** | 50,000 Gold | One-time creation fee in 2026. |

## Managing Guild Ranks and Permissions

Once your guild is established, managing your members effectively is the key to longevity. The 2026 update introduced a more granular permission system, allowing Guild Masters to delegate tasks without risking the security of the guild bank or territory. This **exoria roblox guild guide** recommends a hierarchical structure to maintain order during high-stress events like boss raids or defensive sieges.

The ranking system is fully customizable, but most successful guilds utilize a four-tier system to categorize their player base. Assigning roles based on activity and trust ensures that your faction remains operational even when the Guild Master is offline.

### Standard Rank Hierarchy & Permissions

| Rank Name | Permissions | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Guild Master** | All Permissions | Strategic direction and final decisions. |

Properly utilizing this **exoria roblox guild guide** ranking strategy prevents "insider raiding," where disgruntled members might attempt to empty the guild's shared resources before leaving.

## Guild Progression and Leveling Rewards

Guilds in Exoria earn experience (GXP) through member activity. Every quest completed, boss defeated, and hour spent in-game by a member contributes to the guild's overall level. In 2026, leveling your guild is the only way to unlock the "Ancient Buffs" that provide percentage-based increases to Luck, Damage, and Defense.

As your guild levels up, the capacity for members increases, and you unlock the ability to purchase a Guild Hall. The Guild Hall serves as a private instance where members can rest, trade, and prepare for upcoming battles without the threat of open-world PVP.

## Territory Control and Guild Wars

The most competitive aspect of the **exoria roblox guild guide** involves the Territory Control system. Specific zones across the map, such as the Iron Mines or the Whispering Woods, can be claimed by guilds during scheduled "War Windows." Controlling these areas provides a passive income of resources to the guild bank, which can then be distributed among members.

To claim a territory, a guild must place a Siege Totem in the designated zone and defend it for 30 minutes against rival factions. In 2026, these battles often involve hundreds of players, making coordination via external communication tools like Discord almost mandatory for success.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Losing a territory defense can result in a significant loss of Guild Reputation, which may temporarily disable certain high-level buffs.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I leave a guild in Exoria?**

A: You can leave a guild by opening the Guild Menu (default key 'G'), navigating to the 'Members' tab, and clicking the 'Leave' button next to your name. Note that you will lose all accumulated Guild Contribution points upon leaving.

**Q: Can a Guild Master be changed?**

A: Yes, a Guild Master can transfer leadership to any Officer through the management panel. If a Guild Master is inactive for more than 30 days in 2026, the highest-ranking Officer can petition the High Overseer NPC to take over leadership.

**Q: What happens if our guild bank runs out of gold?**

A: If the guild bank cannot cover the weekly maintenance fees for a Guild Hall or Territory, the benefits will be suspended. If the debt persists for more than seven days, the guild may lose its claimed territory entirely.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many guilds I can join?**

A: According to the current **exoria roblox guild guide** rules, a single account can only be a member of one guild at a time to prevent spying and conflict of interest during territory wars.
