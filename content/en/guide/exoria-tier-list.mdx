## SS Tier: The "Question Mark" Synergy

At the very top of the **exoria tier list** sits a specialized combination that players often refer to as the "Question Mark" build. This isn't a single class you pick from a trainer, but rather the strategic use of Priest abilities while wielding a heavy Hammer.

By combining the Priest's high-intelligence scaling and healing spells with the massive raw damage of the Hammer (specifically the Hellforge Hammer), you create an unstoppable force. The Priest's "Smite" provides a powerful long-range attack, while their healing spells and revival moves ensure you never stay down for long. When you add the Hammer’s ability to knock down and stun enemies, you become a one-man army capable of clearing any boss in the game.

## S Tier: The Heavy Hitters

### Brute (Hammer)
The Brute is widely considered the king of mob farming and bossing. While its abilities might seem simple, the Hammer weapon itself is the star of the show. It features massive knockback and knockdown potential, meaning most enemies will be stunned on the ground before they can even land a hit on you.

### Pikeman (Spear)
The Pikeman excels at safety. Because the Spear has the longest melee range in the game, you can poke enemies from a distance where they cannot retaliate. While it lacks the heavy knockdown of the Hammer, its consistent DPS and safety make it a top-tier choice for players who prefer a "hit and run" tactic.

### Priest
The Priest is much more than a simple healer. With the largest range move in the game (Smite) and the ability to revive fallen teammates, they are mandatory for any serious group content. Interestingly, Priests can use other weapons effectively, allowing them to adapt to various combat situations.

## A Tier: Specialized Combatants

### Rogue (Dagger)
The Rogue is built for speed. If you enjoy high-mobility gameplay and dodging attacks rather than tanking them, this is your class. It requires "Most Armor" or other evasion-based gear to truly shine. However, it lacks crowd control, meaning you must be precise with your combos to avoid being overwhelmed.

### Fighter (Sword)
The Fighter is the most balanced class but often feels like a "weaker Pikeman." It has decent damage and range, but it doesn't excel in any specific category. It is a solid choice for beginners who want a straightforward experience without complex mechanics.

### Mage (Grimoire)
The Mage has incredible damage potential but currently suffers from a lack of specialized "Mage Armor" in the early 2026 patches. Additionally, players must land a physical hit on a mob to receive credit for quests and XP, which can be frustrating for a purely magical class. If you use the Devil Grimoire, your magic damage will skyrocket, but be prepared for a higher skill ceiling.

## Trainer Locations in Exoria

Finding your trainer is the first step to climbing the **exoria tier list**. Most trainers are located within or near the main hub of Falaraga, but some require a bit of a trek.

| Trainer | Location | Directions |

| **Hammer (Brute)** | Guild Hall | Straight ahead from spawn, look for the recruitment building on the left. |

## Gear and Stat Optimization

Your position on the **exoria tier list** is also determined by your gear. Matching the right armor to your class is the difference between an S-tier build and a B-tier struggle.

1.  **Heavy Armor:** Best for Brutes and Fighters. It allows you to trade hits with bosses and stay in the fight longer.
2.  **Evasion Armor:** Essential for Rogues and Brawlers. Since these classes have low health, you must rely on the "Most Armor" sets to dodge incoming damage.
3.  **Intelligence Stats:** Vital for Priests and Mages. This increases your healing potency and spell damage.
4.  **Strength Stats:** The primary focus for Hammers and Spears to maximize the knockdown duration and raw DPS.

## FAQ

**Q: Which class is the best for solo players in Exoria?**

A: The Brute is generally considered the best solo class because the Hammer's knockdown ability prevents enemies from attacking you. This allows you to clear difficult mobs without taking much damage.

**Q: How do I unlock the SS-tier "Question Mark" build?**

A: This is achieved by training as a Priest to unlock healing and Smite abilities, but then equipping a high-tier Hammer (like the Hellforge Hammer) as your primary weapon. This combines the best support skills with the best melee weapon in the game.

**Q: Does the Mage class get XP for spell kills?**

A: Currently, you must land at least one physical hit (fist or weapon) on an enemy to receive XP and gold. If you kill a mob purely with spells, it may not count toward your quest progress. This is a known mechanic that players should keep in mind when climbing the **exoria tier list**.

**Q: Where can I find the best weapons like the Hellforge Hammer?**

A: Rare weapons are typically obtained through high-level boss drops or special crafting recipes found in the later stages of the game. Keep an eye on world boss spawns in the wilderness areas beyond Falaraga.
