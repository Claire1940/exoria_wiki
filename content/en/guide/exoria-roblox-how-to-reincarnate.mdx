## The Fundamentals of Reincarnation

Reincarnation in Exoria is not merely a reset; it is an evolution. When you undergo this process, your base level returns to one, and your quest progress is typically wiped. However, you retain your rare items, cosmetic unlocks, and most importantly, you gain a "Rebirth Tier." Each tier applies a multiplicative bonus to your base stats, meaning that a level 50 player on their third reincarnation might be significantly stronger than a level 100 player who has never reincarnated.

Before you set out to find the ritual site, you must ensure your character meets the strict criteria set by the 2026 update. Attempting to interact with the Reincarnation Altar without these stats will result in a generic dialogue rejection.

## Detailed Steps: Exoria Roblox How to Reincarnate

Follow these steps to complete the ritual:

1.  **Locate the NPC:** Find the NPC named "The Eternal Arbiter" standing near the glowing blue pillar in the Sanctum.
2.  **Clear your Inventory:** Ensure you aren't holding any "Quest Items" that are tied to active missions, as these can occasionally glitch the reset process.
3.  **Initiate Dialogue:** Select the option "I seek a new beginning" when speaking to the Arbiter.

One of the biggest fears players have regarding **exoria roblox how to reincarnate** is the prospect of grinding back to level 500. However, because you retain your high-level gear (assuming you meet the stat requirements to equip it), the second climb is much faster than the first.

*   **Pre-Farm Consumables:** Before you reset, stock up on "Experience Potions" and "Attack Speed Scrolls." These cannot be easily bought at low levels.
*   **Equip Scaling Gear:** Use equipment that provides "XP Gain %" rather than raw damage. Since your base stats are boosted by the reincarnation multiplier, you won't need as much gear-based damage to one-shot early mobs.

## FAQ

**Q: Does reincarnation reset my gold and items?**

A: No, you keep all your gold, equipment, and cosmetic items. Only your character level, quest progress, and unspent stat points are reset. It is highly recommended to spend your stat points before reincarnating to see where your base strengths lie.

**Q: How many times can I reincarnate in Exoria?**

A: As of the 2026 update, there is no hard cap on reincarnations, but the "Stat Multiplier" starts to experience diminishing returns after Tier 10. Most players aim for Tier 5 to unlock all unique traversal abilities.

**Q: Is there a way to skip the level requirement for exoria roblox how to reincarnate?**

A: There is no legitimate way to skip the level requirement. Some limited-time events may lower the requirement to level 450, but generally, you must reach the cap to prove your character's worth to the Eternal Arbiter.

**Q: Where do I find the Essence of Void?**

A: The Essence of Void is a rare drop from the "Void Stalker" boss located in the Shadow Realm. You typically need a group of 3-5 players to take him down, or a very high-level solo build. You need 5 of these every time you wish to reincarnate.
