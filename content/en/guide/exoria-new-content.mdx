## Traversal and the Hoverboard System

Navigating the dark anime world of Exoria is significantly faster thanks to the hoverboard system. While the default board is provided early in the main quest "The Path of Resonance," the **exoria new content** update has added several customizable options and upgrades.

Players can visit the hoverboard shop to purchase new skins or, more importantly, upgrade their board's top speed. This is vital because the render distance in certain curse zones can be low, and being able to outrun a sudden demon spawn is a life-saving mechanic.

*   **Default Board:** Free, balanced stats.
*   **Blazed Board:** Costs 1,000 Crowns, higher base speed.
*   **Game Pass Boards:** Exclusive cosmetics with slight agility boosts.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Demons in the "Whisper Stone Hollow" and other cave areas spawn dynamically as you approach. Ensure your hoverboard is equipped to your hotbar (usually key 3) for a quick escape if you find yourself outnumbered.

## The Demon Dimension and Dungeons

The pinnacle of the current **exoria new content** is the **Demon Dimension**. This is a timed dungeon event that opens at fixed intervals—typically every 30 minutes. Portals appear in the world, allowing groups of players to enter a high-stakes arena filled with "Stronger Demons."

These enemies have significantly higher health pools (often exceeding 200 HP) and utilize their own abilities, such as teleports and area-of-effect blasts. Successfully clearing the Demon Dimension before the portal collapses rewards players with rare materials for the upcoming mining and crafting systems.

### Dungeon Preparation Checklist
1. **Check the Clock:** Portals open at :00 and :30 of every hour.
2. **Equip Your Skills:** Ensure your X and T abilities are slotted in the inventory menu.
3. **Party Up:** While solo play is possible, the Demon Dimension is designed for parties. Use the "Invite Friends" feature in the sidebar.
4. **Health Regen:** Invest points in the "Faster Regen" passive (requires level 45) to minimize downtime between waves.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the X skill in Exoria?**

A: You must reach level 5 and have enough Crowns to purchase the skill from the skill tree menu. Once purchased, you **must** open your inventory and manually equip the skill to the X slot for it to function in combat.

**Q: What is the benefit of the Demon race in the exoria new content update?**

A: The Demon race is a "Rare" pull that provides higher base damage and a unique transformation during your "True Awakening" (V skill). It also changes your character's aesthetic, often giving them a more aggressive, supernatural appearance compared to the Human race.

**Q: How often do the Demon Dimension portals open?**

A: Portals to the Demon Dimension typically open every 30 minutes at fixed times. You will see a global notification or a portal icon on your map when the event is active. Ensure you are near a major landmark to find the entrance quickly.

**Q: Can I play Exoria on mobile?**

A: Yes, Exoria is fully compatible with PC, mobile, and console. The UI scales to your device, though many players find the "Shift Lock" and "Dash Cancelling" mechanics easier to execute with a keyboard or controller.
