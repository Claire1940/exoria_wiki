## Choosing Your Path: Magic Class Overview

In the current state of Exoria, magic classes offer some of the highest utility and damage potential. Choosing between a Priest and a Mage significantly alters your stat priorities and your role in group play. Both classes require a significant investment in gold to unlock their full potential, with each skill costing approximately 2,500 gold.

### The Priest Class
The Priest is the backbone of any successful raiding party. Their primary focus is on the Healing Bonus stat, which dictates the effectiveness of their restorative spells. If you prefer a supportive role where you keep your allies alive during grueling boss encounters, the Priest is your best bet.

### The Mage Class
For those who want to see massive numbers on their screen, the Mage is the premier DPS option. Mages scale primarily with Magic Damage percentage boosts. Their skills are designed for area-of-effect (AoE) destruction and high-burst single-target damage, making them ideal for soloing content if you have the right artifacts.

## Essential Artifacts and Stat Boosts

Artifacts are the most critical component of any **exoria stat guide**. Unlike standard gear, these items provide permanent or semi-permanent buffs that drastically change your power level. In 2026, three main artifacts dominate the endgame meta.

### The Devil's Grimoire
This is the holy grail for Mage players. It provides a flat 20% boost to Magic Damage. When combined with racial bonuses (such as the 10% boost found in certain races), a player can reach a 30% total magic damage increase. Note that once equipped, this artifact is difficult to swap, so ensure you are committed to a magic-heavy build before using it.

### Angel's Genesis
The Angel's Genesis is the defensive and supportive counterpart to the Grimoire. It provides a massive +250 Healing Bonus. This is essential for Priests who want to ensure their heals can outpace the damage dealt by endgame bosses.

### The Life Bonus Artifact
Rare and elusive, this artifact grants the user an additional life. In a game where death can be punishing, having an extra buffer is invaluable. However, it is a single-use item that disappears upon consumption, so it should be saved for the most difficult challenges.

| Artifact | Primary Benefit | Drop Source | Rarity |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Devil's Grimoire** | +20% Magic Damage | Firestorm Boss / Orc Boss | Very Rare |
| **Angel's Genesis** | +250 Healing Bonus | Firestorm Boss | Rare |
| **Life Bonus** | +1 Life (Consumable) | Firestorm Boss | Ultra Rare |

> **Warning:** Artifact drop rates are notoriously low. Some players report grinding for over 8 hours even during 50% drop rate events without seeing a Devil's Grimoire. Patience is key.

## Boss Farming for Stat Progression

To obtain the artifacts mentioned in this **exoria stat guide**, you must master the art of boss farming. There are two primary bosses that players should focus on depending on their current level and gear.

### The Orc Boss (Early-Mid Game)
Located near the Orc encampment, this boss is the entry point for artifact hunting. While the drop rates are lower than the final boss, he can be soloed even by low-level players using specific environmental strategies.

*   **The Pillar Method:** Position yourself behind the stone pillars near the boss. By using a weapon with reach, such as a spear, you can hit the boss while his AI struggles to navigate around the terrain.
*   **Solo Potential:** Even a level 1 player can theoretically defeat this boss with enough time and a spear, making it a great way to jumpstart your progression.

### The Firestorm Boss (Endgame)
This is currently the most difficult encounter in Exoria. He is located deep within the volcanic region and requires careful navigation to reach.

1.  **Preparation:** Use your CER (Combat Enhancement Radar) to maintain visibility in the dark corridors leading to the boss.
2.  **The Elevator:** Avoid jumping straight into the pit. Use the lever at the top to bring the elevator down safely to avoid being ambushed by "zombie" players or mobs at the bottom.
3.  **The Glitch Strategy:** If the boss is too difficult to fight fairly, you can lure him to the ledge. When he performs his "Thrust" ability, he may become stuck in the geometry, allowing you to safely chip away at his health from below.

## Optimal Build Paths for 2026

When building your character, you should align your stats with your chosen artifacts. Mixing physical and magical stats is generally discouraged in the current meta as it dilutes your effectiveness in high-level raids.

### The "Glass Cannon" Mage Build
*   **Core Stat:** Magic Damage %
*   **Required Artifact:** Devil's Grimoire
*   **Strategy:** Focus on unlocking the Fireball skill early. Use the "Fireball and Kite" method to deal damage from a distance while avoiding direct hits. This build excels at the Firestorm Boss if you can maintain your distance.

### The "Immortal" Priest Build
*   **Core Stat:** Healing Bonus / HP
*   **Required Artifact:** Angel's Genesis
*   **Strategy:** Prioritize skills that provide AoE healing. In group play, your goal is to stay behind the tanks and keep the "Healing Bonus" stat as high as possible to ensure one-shot mechanics don't wipe your team.

> **Tip:** If you are struggling to find artifacts, join the official [Exoria Discord](https://www.roblox.com/games/7253328431/Exoria) to find groups for farming. Cooperation significantly speeds up the process.

## Stat Scaling and Race Synergies

Your choice of race in Exoria provides a foundational layer to your stats that shouldn't be ignored. While artifacts provide the largest raw numbers, racial passives act as multipliers.

*   **Magic-Inclined Races:** Look for races that offer a base 10% Magic Damage boost. When paired with the Devil's Grimoire, this allows you to hit the 30% threshold, which is a major breakpoint for one-shotting common mobs.
*   **Tank-Inclined Races:** If you are building a Priest, look for races with health regeneration or base health increases to complement your healing output.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find an exoria stat guide for specific skill trees?**

A: The best way to view skill trees is to visit the trainers directly. The Mage trainer is located on the tower, and the Priest trainer is near the village. Each skill will cost you 2,500 gold, so save up before visiting.

**Q: Can I equip more than one artifact at a time?**

A: No, you generally have one primary artifact slot for items like the Devil's Grimoire or Angel's Genesis. Using a new one will typically overwrite or prevent the use of the other, so choose the one that fits your build best.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up my stats in Exoria?**

A: Farming the Orc boss using the pillar method is the most efficient way for new players to gain experience and potential artifact drops without needing high-end gear.

**Q: Does the Pirate Mini-Boss drop artifacts?**

A: Most community data suggests that the Pirate Mini-Boss does not drop the top-tier artifacts like the Devil's Grimoire. Stick to the Orc Boss or the Firestorm Boss for the best results.
