## Getting Started: Character Customization and Controls

Before you dive into the demon-infested streets, you must set up your character. A common hurdle for new players is the customization system. Currently, the interface is most stable on mobile devices. If you are playing on a console or PC and find the customization menus unresponsive, try logging in via a mobile device to finalize your appearance before returning to your preferred platform.

### Essential Controls for Exoria
Understanding the UI is half the battle. Many players overlook the quest button or struggle with the camera lock-on features.

| Action | Control (PC) | Control (Console) |
|---|---|---|
| **Shift Lock** | Shift Key / Toggle on Side | Right Stick Click / Toggle |
| **Block** | F Key | L1 / LB |
| **Dash/Slide** | Q Key | L2 / LT |
| **Open Quest Log** | UI Icon (Bottom Right) | D-Pad Down |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep Shift Lock enabled during combat. The lack of a traditional hard lock-on system means you must manually aim your attacks and air combos to remain effective against agile demons.

## Choosing Your Class: Sword vs. Aria

One of the most critical decisions in your journey is selecting your starting class. When you arrive at the Academy and speak to Yukio, you will be presented with two primary paths: the Sword Class and the Aria Class.

### The Sword Class
The Sword Class is the most popular choice for players who enjoy high-intensity, close-quarters combat. It focuses on physical damage, air combos, and rapid executions. This class is highly effective for soloing dungeons but requires precise timing for blocks and parries.

### The Aria Class
The Aria Class (Path of Resonance) utilizes chants and spiritual energy to deal damage from a distance or provide utility. While historically considered more difficult to level in the early game, the Aria Class excels in group play and "Train Events" where crowd control is essential.

| Class | Combat Style | Difficulty | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sword** | Melee / Combo-based | Easy | Solo Progression |
| **Aria** | Ranged / Chanting | Medium | Group Dungeons |

## Navigating the World of Exoria

The map in Exoria is divided into several key zones, including the City, the Subway, the Academy, and the Big Cave. To move between these areas quickly, utilize the hoverboard provided to new players or look for fast-travel NPCs located near the main hubs.

### The Academy and Yukio
Your progression is tied to the True Cross Academy. Speaking to Yukio is mandatory to unlock your class and receive your initial training missions. If you find yourself lost without a clear objective, return to the Academy to see if a new "Class Session" has started.

### The Subway and Train Events
A "Mysterious Train" occasionally appears in the Subway zone. This is a timed event where waves of demons (including Brutes) spawn. Clearing these waves provides a significant boost to your experience points and purity bar.

## Resource Gathering: Mining in the Big Cave

To upgrade your equipment and progress through certain questlines, you will need to gather raw materials. The "Big Cave" is the primary location for resource extraction. This area contains various ore veins that can be mined for Crystals and Diamonds.

### Mining Locations and Drops
Not all rocks are created equal. You should look for specific nodes to maximize your efficiency.

| Resource | Node Appearance | Common Location |
|---|---|---|
| **Crystals** | Black-tinted rocks | Cave Entrance / Lower Levels |
| **Diamonds** | Glowing blue veins | Deep Cave / Near Boss Arena |
| **Soul Shards** | Purple crystalline structures | Rare Spawn in Cave |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Big Cave is also home to Level 60+ demons. If you are a low-level player, stick to the nodes near the entrance and avoid wandering too deep into the darker tunnels until you have unlocked your first two combat skills.

## Advanced Combat Mechanics

As you progress through this **exoria roblox guide**, you will notice your "Purity Bar" changing. This bar represents your alignment and power level. Engaging in "Executions"—special finishing moves triggered when an enemy is at low health—will help fill this bar faster and unlock powerful state transformations.

### Mastery of Skills
Every class has a set of core skills that must be mastered. For the Sword class, these often revolve around fire-based impacts.

*   **Soul Convergence:** A utility skill that pulls enemies closer or stabilizes your spiritual energy.
*   **Infernal Impact:** A high-damage AOE (Area of Effect) attack that creates a burst of blue flames.
*   **Radio Burst:** A fast-acting projectile or dash attack used to close the gap between you and a fleeing demon.

### Defeating Malphas
Malphas is one of the first major hurdles for players reaching Level 15-20. This boss resides within the dungeon system and requires a mix of air combos and skill rotations to defeat.

1.  **Dodge the Initial Charge:** Malphas always starts the fight with a heavy lunge.
2.  **Chip Damage:** Use your basic M1 (Left Click) string to build up your skill meter.
3.  **Burst Window:** Once Malphas finishes a heavy attack animation, unleash **Infernal Impact** followed by **Soul Convergence**.
4.  **Execution:** When the prompt appears, press the interact key to finish the fight and earn massive XP.

## Leveling Strategy for 2026

To reach the level cap quickly, follow this optimized path:
1.  **Levels 1-10:** Focus on Yukio’s basic quests and killing "Weak Demons" around the Academy.
2.  **Levels 11-25:** Participate in every Subway Train Event that pops up. The mob density here is the best for XP farming.
3.  **Levels 26-50:** Start farming the Big Cave dungeons. Even if you cannot solo Malphas yet, joining a public party will grant you shared experience.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I fix the customization bug in Exoria?**

A: If you cannot customize your character on a console or PC, log into the game using a mobile device. The mobile UI is currently more optimized for the character editor. Once saved, your appearance will carry over to other platforms.

**Q: Where can I find the Big Cave for the mining quest?**

A: The Big Cave is located on the outskirts of the main city. Look for the large mountain formation. It is often marked by a quest waypoint once you reach the appropriate level in this **exoria roblox guide**.

**Q: What does the Purity Bar do?**

A: The Purity Bar tracks your spiritual resonance. As it fills, your skills deal more damage, and you eventually unlock the ability to perform high-tier Executions on elite demons and bosses.

**Q: Is there a way to fast travel in Exoria?**

A: Yes, you can speak to specific NPCs (like Yukio or Liren) who provide transport to key locations. Additionally, the hoverboard item allows for significantly faster movement across the open world map.

**Q: Why isn't my Aria skill working?**

A: Aria skills often require a "Chant Time." Ensure you are not being hit while the skill is activating, as taking damage can interrupt the resonance and cancel the ability.
