## Getting Started: Character Creation and Classes

When you first launch the game, you are greeted with a detailed character customization menu. Beyond just aesthetics like hair color and accessories, your most important choice during the early game is your Race. Currently, players can roll for different races, which provide various stat bonuses and influence your "Awakened" form later in the game.

| Race | Rarity | Primary Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Human** | Common | Balanced stats, standard progression path. |
| **Demon** | Rare | Increased power, unique red transformation aesthetics. |

After finalizing your look, you will enter the tutorial area where you must choose your Path of Resonance. This determines your playstyle for the foreseeable future.

*   **Sword Class:** Focuses on fast, flashy melee combat. It utilizes high-mobility slashes and powerful ground-slams to overwhelm enemies.
*   **Aria Class:** A magic-based path that relies on incantations and area-of-effect (AoE) spells to control the battlefield from a distance.

> **💡 Tip:** If you prefer high-intensity action and parrying mechanics, the Sword Class is highly recommended for beginners due to its intuitive M1 combo strings.

## Mastering the Combat System

The combat in **Roblox Exoria** is designed to be fluid and responsive, supporting PC, mobile, and console play. To succeed against stronger demons, you must master more than just clicking your mouse.

### Core Controls and Mechanics
1.  **M1 (Left Click):** Basic attack string.
2.  **Right Click:** Block breaker; essential for opening up defensive enemies.
3.  **Space (During Combo):** Initiates air combat, allowing you to take the fight into the sky.
4.  **F Key:** Parrying and blocking. Timed blocks can negate damage and stun opponents.
5.  **Q Key:** Dashing. You can use dash-canceling to extend combos or escape AOE attacks.

### The Skill Tree
As you level up, you will earn points to spend in your skill tree. Skills are categorized into Combat Mastery, Crafting, and Gathering. Note that many powerful abilities have both a level requirement and a **Purity** requirement.

| Skill Name | Type | Level Required | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wind Flurry (Z)** | Active | 1 | A violent flurry of slashing wind strikes. |
| **Earth Splitter (X)** | Active | 5 | A heavy crushing slash that sends enemies flying. |
| **Special Finisher** | Passive | 15 | Triggers a unique cutscene when an enemy is low HP. |
| **HP Regeneration** | Passive | 45 | Significantly increases passive health recovery. |

## Understanding the Purity System

One of the standout features of **Roblox Exoria** is the Purity Bar, located at the bottom of your screen. This bar tracks your moral alignment based on the spirits you "grip" or purify.

*   **Positive Purity (Orange):** Earned by purifying floating spirits and lost souls. High purity can attract certain types of enemies but is required for "Light" path skills.
*   **Negative Purity (Purple):** Earned by embracing darker powers or gripping specific entities. This path unlocks "Shadow" abilities and different cosmetic auras.

Your purity level is not just a cosmetic stat; it acts as a gatekeeper for the skill tree. For example, some high-level transformations require at least 20 Purity to activate. Always check your quest log to see if a mission rewards specific purity points.

## Update 2: The Train Event and Ether Relics

The latest update to **Roblox Exoria** has introduced a variety of endgame activities and quality-of-life improvements. The most significant addition is the **Train Event**, a timed challenge that tests the coordination of the entire server.

### How the Train Event Works
The Train Event spawns every 30 minutes and remains active for 15 minutes. Players must defend against three increasingly difficult waves of demons.
*   **Wave 1:** Basic spirits and weak demons.
*   **Wave 2:** Medium-tier demons with teleportation abilities.
*   **Wave 3:** Elite demons with high HP and guard-breaking moves.

Completing this event rewards players with massive amounts of XP, coins, and the exclusive **Ether Relics**.

### The Merchant and Accessories
Ether Relics are a premium currency used at the specialized Merchant NPC. Here, you can purchase exclusive accessories that provide stat boosts not found elsewhere in the game. Additionally, the Merchant allows you to sell unwanted items obtained from boss dungeons, making it a vital hub for inventory management.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Items are now stackable as of Update 2, but be sure to lock your most valuable gear to prevent accidental selling at the Merchant!

## Exploration and World Activities

Beyond combat, the world of **Roblox Exoria** offers several ways to progress your character. Exploring areas like the Whisper Stone Hollow or the Exorcist Academy will reveal secrets and new quest-givers.

### Hoverboards and Mobility
To traverse the map quickly, players can use Hoverboards. The default board is functional, but you can upgrade to the **Blazed Board** or other premium versions using credits or Robux.
*   **Speed Upgrades:** You can spend crowns/credits to increase the base speed of your board.
*   **Customization:** Higher-tier boards offer unique trail effects and better handling.

### Mining and Crafting
Equipping a pickaxe allows you to interact with ore veins found in caves and mountainous regions. While the crafting system is still evolving in early access, mining is currently the best way to gather materials for future gear upgrades and weekly quests.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock new skills in Roblox Exoria?**

A: Skills are unlocked via the Skill Tree menu. You must meet the minimum level requirement and often a specific Purity threshold. Once a skill is purchased, you must manually equip it to your hotbar (Z, X, C, V keys) to use it in combat.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up?**

A: The fastest way to gain XP is by completing the Main Questline, followed by participating in the Train Event every 30 minutes. Daily and Weekly quests also provide significant XP chunks and free race rerolls.

**Q: Can I play Roblox Exoria on mobile?**

A: Yes, the game is fully optimized for PC, Mobile, and Console. The UI scales automatically, and touch controls are available for all combat abilities and hoverboard movement.

**Q: What are Ether Relics used for?**

A: Ether Relics are a special currency earned from the Train Event and high-level dungeons. They are used at the Merchant NPC to buy exclusive, high-tier accessories that cannot be obtained through standard gameplay or drops.
