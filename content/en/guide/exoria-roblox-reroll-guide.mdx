## How to Farm Resources for Rerolling

To effectively use an **exoria roblox reroll guide**, you need a steady stream of currency. Update 2 introduced several new ways to gather the materials required for high-end character resets.

### The Train Event
The Train Event is the most lucrative activity for players seeking Ether Relics. 
1. **Timing:** The event spawns every 30 minutes and lasts for 15 minutes.
2. **Waves:** You must clear three waves of enemies.
3. **Scaling:** The difficulty scales with the number of players, but so does the speed of the clear.
4. **Rewards:** Completing all three waves yields massive XP, Coins, and the coveted Ether Relics.

### Soul Farming for Skills
Souls are the backbone of the Exoria economy. You will need them not just for rerolling, but for unlocking mobility skills that complement your new build. For instance, the **Advanced Dash** is a critical skill that costs 7,500 Souls.

To find the Advanced Dash trainer:
1. Locate the Guild building.
2. Cross the main bridge and head underneath the secondary bridge.
3. Look for a white bridge structure where an NPC resides.
4. Exchange 7,500 Souls to learn the skill.

> **Warning:** Resetting your character or "rerolling" certain aspects may occasionally wipe your equipped skills if you are not careful. Always double-check the NPC's dialogue before confirming a full reset.

## Step-by-Step Reroll Strategy

Follow these steps to maximize the efficiency of your **exoria roblox reroll guide** journey:

1. **Accumulate Wealth:** Do not start rerolling until you have at least 50,000 Souls and 20 Ether Relics. Rerolling with a low budget often leaves you with a worse trait than you started with.
2. **Visit the Merchant:** Use your Ether Relics at the specialized merchant to buy reroll tokens or exclusive accessories that boost your "Luck" stat.
3. **Identify Your Goal:** Decide if you are rerolling for a specific Race (visual and stat changes) or a Trait (purely stat-based).
4. **The "Soft Cap" Rule:** If you hit an A-tier trait like **Iron-Clad** and have fewer than 10 rerolls left, stop. It is better to have a strong A-tier than to run out of currency and get stuck with a C-tier "Sturdy" trait.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I reroll my character without spending Robux?**

A: Yes, this **exoria roblox reroll guide** focuses on free-to-play methods. You can obtain reroll materials by participating in the Train Event every 30 minutes and completing the new daily and weekly quests added in Update 2.

**Q: What happens to my Advanced Dash if I reroll my race?**

A: Generally, skills purchased with Souls, like the Advanced Dash, stay with your character even if you reroll your race or traits. However, always ensure you have saved your progress at the Guild before making major changes.

**Q: Is the Train Event too hard for solo players?**

A: While the waves get harder with more players, the event is designed to be cleared faster with a group. Solo players can clear it, but it requires a high-tier build (S-Tier or A-Tier) and a good supply of stackable healing items.

**Q: Where is the best place to sell items for Coins?**

A: You can sell items obtained from boss dungeons at the Merchant. This is a great way to clear your inventory of non-stackable legacy items and fund your next round of rerolls.
