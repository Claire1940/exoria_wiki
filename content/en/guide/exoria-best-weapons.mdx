## Ranking the Exoria Best Weapons by Tier

The weapon progression in Exoria is strictly divided into three distinct tiers. While Tier 1 weapons are easily accessible via the main town shops, Tier 3 weapons require significant parkour skills and the bravery to enter high-level dungeons where a single fall can result in the loss of a life slot.

### Tier 1: The Foundation Weapons
Tier 1 weapons are your entry point into the game's combat system. Most of these are found within the starting town, primarily around the "Fighting Chance" shop and the Guild Hall.

| Weapon Type | Location | Key Feature | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sword** | Fighting Chance Shop | Balanced Stats | Beginners / All-rounders |
| **Dagger** | Fighting Chance Shop | High Attack Speed | Stealth & Quick Bursts |
| **Hammer** | Guild Hall (Town) | High Stun/Damage | Heavy Hitters |
| **Spear** | Near Golem Field | Extreme Range | Safe PVE Farming |

> **💡 Tip:** The Spear is widely considered the best Tier 1 weapon for leveling because its range allows you to hit Golems and other mobs without taking return damage.

### Tier 2: The Coral Series
Mid-game players should migrate toward the Goblin Camp area. Deep within this territory lies a small hidden city housing a Blacksmith who sells the Coral weapon set. These are a significant step up from the base Tier 1 gear and are necessary for surviving the transition to the Sky Castle.

To find these, head toward the Goblin Camp and follow the path toward the Fist Trainer. Instead of ascending the mountain, continue straight into the valley until you see the stone structures of the hidden city.

## Deep Dive: Tier 3 and End-Game Gear

The **exoria best weapons** in the current 2026 meta are exclusively found in the Sky Castle / Castellan dungeon. Reaching this area requires using a massive wooden elevator platform found near the Sky Castle outskirts.

## Class Upgrades and Transformations

In 2026, the "Sword Class" has received a massive overhaul, introducing upgraded skills that vary based on your character's race. These upgrades are what truly define the **exoria best weapons** at the end-game level.

### Demon vs. Non-Demon Forms
The weapon you hold is only as good as the form you take. Upgraded sword skills now feature unique transformation cutscenes:
*   **Non-Demon Forms:** Focus on purity and high-speed blade work. These forms typically offer better defensive buffs and parry windows.
*   **Demon Forms:** Focus on raw destructive power and area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. These are preferred for clearing large groups of enemies in the Sky Castle.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Transformations are high-commitment moves. Ensure you are in a safe position before triggering the cutscene, as you may be vulnerable to environmental hazards during the transition.

## Weapon Archetype Analysis

To choose between the **exoria best weapons**, you must understand the hidden stats that aren't always visible in the shop menu.

### The Spear: The King of Range
The Spear remains a fan favorite in 2026 because of its safety. In high-stakes areas like the Castellan dungeon, being able to poke enemies from a distance prevents you from being knocked off ledges. While its damage per hit is lower than the Hammer, its "DPS" (Damage Per Second) is competitive due to the speed of its thrusts.

### The Hammer: Heavy Impact
The Tier 3 Hammer, found in the secret lower section of the dungeon, is the only weapon that comes with a dedicated armor set specifically designed to boost its impact. If you enjoy a "Tank" playstyle, this is the definitive choice. It can break guards more easily than any other weapon type.

### The Sword: The Skill Expression Choice
With the 2026 upgrades, the Sword is no longer just a "basic" weapon. The new skill movesets allow for complex combos that can lock an opponent in place. For players who have mastered the timing of the game's combat, the Tier 3 Sword offers the highest potential for outplaying opponents in PVP.

## How to Optimize Your Build

1.  **Tier 1 Phase:** Purchase the Spear immediately. Use it to farm Golems until you have enough currency for the full Tier 1 set.
2.  **Tier 2 Transition:** Head to the Goblin Camp. Do not engage the goblins if you are under-leveled; simply run past them to the hidden city Blacksmith.
3.  **Tier 3 Preparation:** Before heading to the Sky Castle, ensure you have at least one mobility skill. The elevator and the dungeon walkways are unforgiving.
4.  **Race Synergy:** If you are a Demon race, prioritize the Sword to take advantage of the high-damage transformation skills.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find the exoria best weapons for beginners?**

A: For beginners, the best weapons are found in the starting town. The Sword and Dagger are located in the "Fighting Chance" shop near the Blacksmith, while the Hammer is in the Guild Hall. The Spear, which is highly recommended for early leveling, is located near the Golem fields next to the Spear Trainer.

**Q: How do I reach the Tier 3 weapon locations?**

A: Tier 3 weapons are located in the Sky Castle (Castellan) dungeon. You must find the large wooden structure with an elevator platform. Use the lever to bring the platform down, ride it to the top, and navigate the dark dungeon corridors. Be careful not to fall, as it will cost you a life slot.

**Q: Are Demon forms better than Non-Demon forms for swords?**

A: Neither is strictly "better," as they serve different purposes. Demon forms generally provide more AoE and raw damage, making them great for PVE. Non-Demon forms often provide better utility and speed, which many top-tier PVP players prefer for 1v1 encounters.

**Q: What is the most important stat for end-game weapons in Exoria?**

A: While damage is important, "Range" and "Speed" are often more critical in end-game areas like the Sky Castle. Because the terrain is so dangerous, being able to hit enemies before they get close enough to knock you back is vital for survival.
