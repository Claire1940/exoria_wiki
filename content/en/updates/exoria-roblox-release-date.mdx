## New Content in Update 2

Following the successful launch, Update 2 has introduced significant gameplay loops that encourage player cooperation. The centerpiece of this update is the Train Event, a timed challenge that rewards those who can withstand escalating waves of enemies.

### The Train Event Mechanics
The Train Event is a dynamic world occurrence that tests your combat prowess. It spawns every 30 minutes and remains active for a 15-minute window. Players must work together to clear three distinct waves of enemies.

*   **Scaling Difficulty:** Each wave is progressively harder than the last.
*   **Co-op Bonus:** The more players present, the faster the waves can be cleared.
*   **Exclusive Rewards:** Earn XP, Coins, and the rare **Ether Relics**.

### The Merchant and Ether Relics

Additionally, the Merchant now allows players to sell surplus items gathered from boss dungeons, providing a much-needed gold sink and inventory management solution.

## Core Gameplay Mechanics

Exoria is not just about mindless clicking. It incorporates several systems that reward exploration and moral choices. Your path is defined by how you interact with the spirits and objects found within the forest.

### Purity and Curses
Players can choose to purify lost souls or remove curses from haunted objects. These actions grant XP but, more importantly, they affect your **Purity Rating**.
*   **Positive Purity:** Gained by choosing the "righteous" path; unlocks specific skills in the skill tree earlier.
*   **Negative Purity:** Gained through darker choices; provides a different progression path and unique skill unlocks.

### Mining and Upgrades
To increase your damage output, you must upgrade your weapons. This requires specific "parts" obtained through the mining system. As you mine more frequently, you unlock higher mining tiers, allowing you to harvest rarer materials more efficiently.

## Questing System

To keep the gameplay loop fresh, Exoria utilizes a tiered questing system. This ensures that players always have a goal to work toward, whether they have five minutes or five hours to play.

1.  **Main Quests:** Drive the narrative forward and unlock new areas of the map.
2.  **Daily Quests:** Short-term tasks that provide a steady stream of XP and basic currency.
3.  **Weekly Quests:** More difficult challenges, often involving dungeon clears or boss kills, offering rare rewards.
4.  **Purity Quests:** Specialized tasks found in Purity Corridors that shift your moral alignment.

> **Tip:** Always check your "Free Rewards" tab as you level up. Many players forget to claim these bonuses, which include essential upgrade materials and hoverboard parts.
