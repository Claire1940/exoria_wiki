## The New Train Event: Mechanics and Rewards

The centerpiece of **exoria roblox update 2** is undoubtedly the Train Event. This world event is designed to bring the community together, offering high-intensity combat and lucrative rewards for those who can withstand the pressure. Unlike standard dungeons, the Train Event operates on a strict schedule and requires efficient wave management.

### Event Schedule and Participation
The Train Event spawns every **30 minutes** and remains active for a total of **15 minutes**. This frequent rotation ensures that players across all time zones have multiple opportunities to participate throughout their gaming session. When the event begins, players must navigate to the designated area to begin clearing waves of enemies.

### Wave Breakdown
The event consists of three distinct waves. While the presence of more players allows for faster clearing of these waves, the difficulty scales significantly as you progress. 

| Wave Number | Difficulty Level | Primary Reward Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wave 1** | Low | Basic Coins & XP |
| **Wave 2** | Medium | Increased XP & Coin Multiplier |
| **Wave 3** | High | **Ether Relics** & Massive XP |

> **💡 Tip:** To clear the event as efficiently as possible, team up with other players. While the enemies get tougher, the collective damage output of a group far outweighs the scaling health pools of the mobs.

## Mastering the Merchant and Ether Relics

A major addition in **exoria roblox update 2** is the introduction of a specialized Merchant NPC. This character serves as the primary gold and relic sink for high-level players, providing access to gear that cannot be found anywhere else in the game world.

### What are Ether Relics?
Ether Relics are the new premium currency introduced in this update. Unlike standard coins, which can be earned through most activities, Ether Relics are primarily obtained by successfully completing the Train Event. These relics are the only way to purchase the Merchant's "Exclusive Accessories."

### Trading and Economy
The Merchant isn't just for buying; he also offers a way to clean up your inventory. You can now sell items acquired from other dungeons, such as the Boss Dungeon, directly to the Merchant. This provides a consistent way to turn unwanted loot into usable currency.

| Merchant Interaction | Requirement | Benefit |

| **Buy Accessories** | Ether Relics | High-stat exclusive gear |
| **Sell Dungeon Loot** | Rare/Epic Items | Rapid Coin accumulation |
| **Currency Exchange** | Varies | Strategy-dependent |
