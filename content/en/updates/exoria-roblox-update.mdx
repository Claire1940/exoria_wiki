## The New Train Event: Waves and Rewards

One of the most anticipated additions in the recent **exoria roblox update** is the Train Event. This timed challenge serves as a primary source for high-tier currency and exclusive items. Unlike standard field farming, the Train Event requires coordination and speed to maximize the rewards.

The event operates on a strict schedule, spawning every 30 minutes and remaining active for a 15-minute window. During this time, players must clear three distinct waves of enemies. A unique scaling mechanic is in place: the more players participating in the event, the faster the waves can be cleared. However, don't expect a walk in the park—each subsequent wave increases in difficulty, testing your mastery of block-breaking and ability timing.

| Wave Number | Difficulty Level | Primary Rewards | Key Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Wave 1** | Normal | XP, Base Coins | Clear minor spirits quickly |
| **Wave 2** | Hard | Increased XP, Coins | Defeat armored demon variants |
| **Wave 3** | Elite | Massive XP, **Ether Relics** | Survive the boss-tier wave |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check your inventory before the event starts. With items now being stackable as of the latest update, you can carry significantly more consumables to sustain yourself through the Elite wave.

## The Merchant and Ether Relics

The introduction of Ether Relics has shifted the game's economy. These relics are not just another currency; they are the gatekeepers to the most prestigious equipment in the game. Once you have successfully farmed Ether Relics from the Train Event, you must visit the specialized Merchant.

The Merchant offers exclusive accessories that cannot be obtained through standard dungeons or boss drops. This creates a dedicated gameplay loop where players must participate in world events to refine their character's build. Additionally, the Merchant serves as a "buy-back" and trade hub, allowing you to sell unwanted items gathered from the Boss Dungeon to clear up space and earn extra capital.

## The Purity System and Demon Dimension

Progression in Exoria is not just about level; it is about your soul's resonance. The Purity Bar, located at the bottom of your HUD, tracks your actions. Purifying floating spirits grants positive purity (Orange), while "gripping" certain NPCs or embracing darker powers shifts the bar toward the negative (Purple).

This system is dynamic. High purity might make you a beacon for certain demon spirits, while a negative alignment could unlock darker, more destructive versions of your class abilities. The **exoria roblox update** has also introduced the Demon Dimension Portal. This portal opens at fixed intervals (e.g., 12:30, 13:00). Players must enter and defeat the inhabitants before the portal collapses, offering a high-risk, high-reward alternative to standard questing.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Entering the Demon Dimension solo is highly discouraged for players under Level 15. The enemies inside utilize flash-steps and teleportation mechanics that can easily overwhelm an unprepared exorcist.

## Combat Mechanics and Hidden Secrets

The combat in Exoria relies on a mix of M1 (Left Click) attacks, Block Breakers (Right Click), and Air Combat (Space during combo). Mastering the "Dash Cancel" is vital for high-level PvP and boss encounters. The blue bar on your HUD represents your Combat Breaking potential; ensure it is full before attempting to interrupt a boss's ultimate move.

The **exoria roblox update** has also added two new main quests, a daily quest, and a weekly quest. These are essential for farming rerolls. Rerolls allow you to change your race (Human or Demon), with the Demon race currently sitting at a "Rare" tier, providing unique passive buffs and a distinct appearance during Awakening.

## FAQ

**Q: How often does the Train Event happen in the Exoria Roblox update?**

A: The Train Event spawns every 30 minutes and lasts for a duration of 15 minutes. It is the best way to earn Ether Relics for exclusive accessories.

**Q: What is the difference between positive and negative purity?**

A: Positive purity (orange) is gained by purifying spirits, while negative purity (purple) is gained through darker actions. Your purity level can affect which enemies are attracted to you and which skills you can unlock in the tree.

**Q: Can I play Exoria on mobile and console?**

A: Yes, Exoria is fully compatible with PC, Mobile, and Console. The UI and keybinds are optimized for each platform, including full controller support.

**Q: How do I unlock the Awakening form?**

A: Awakening is typically the final skill in your class tree, requiring Level 50 and a high amount of Purity. The appearance of the Awakening form changes based on whether your race is Human or Demon.
