## Character Creation and Race Selection

When you first enter the world, your journey begins with character customization and the critical "Race Roll." Currently, the game features two primary races that dictate your initial stats and aesthetic during transformations.

| Race | Rarity | Primary Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Human** | Common | Balanced stats; standard progression path. |
| **Demon** | Rare | Increased potential for **Dark Purity**; unique transformation visuals. |

While many players aim for the Demon race due to its rarity and thematic ties to the source material, starting as a Human is perfectly viable for all early-game content. Players receive five free rolls at the start, and additional rerolls can be earned by completing daily and weekly quests or by finding random spirits in the world that grant rewards.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't burn all your Robux on rerolls immediately. Daily quests are a consistent way to earn free rerolls while you level up.

## Master the Mechanics of Exoria Roblox Early Access

The combat in Exoria is designed to be fast-paced and flashy, utilizing a mix of traditional melee strikes and supernatural abilities. Understanding your keybinds is essential for surviving encounters with "Weak Demons" and eventually tackling bosses like Marvel.

### Combat Controls and Maneuvers

| Keybind | Action | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **LMB (M1)** | Light Attack | Standard sword slash; can be chained into combos. |
| **RMB (M2)** | Block Breaker | Heavy attack used to bypass an enemy's guard. |
| **Space (Mid-combo)** | Air Combat | Launches the enemy for aerial strikes. |
| **F** | Block/Parry | Mitigates damage; timing it perfectly triggers a parry. |
| **Q + Direction** | Dash | Essential for dodging area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. |
| **L-Control** | Shift Lock | Toggles the camera for better combat precision. |

### Choosing Your Class

Once you reach the Exorcist School, you must choose between two primary paths: the **Sword Class** or the **Area Class**. 

1.  **Sword Class**: Focuses on high-speed physical strikes and "Satan's Blade" style abilities. It is ideal for players who prefer close-quarters combat and rapid-fire combos.
2.  **Area Class**: Likely focuses on magic-based or wide-range abilities, allowing for better crowd control against multiple spirits.

## The Purity System: Light vs. Shadow

One of the most defining features of the **exoria roblox early access** experience is the Purity bar. Located at the bottom of the screen, this bar tracks your moral alignment based on your actions in the world.

*   **Positive Purity (Orange)**: Gained by purifying floating spirits and lost souls. High positive purity may attract different types of enemies or unlock specific "Light" skills in the skill tree.
*   **Negative Purity (Purple)**: Gained by "gripping" (finishing off) certain NPCs or choosing the path of shadow. This unlocks darker, more aggressive abilities.

Your choice of purity affects which skills you can unlock and how certain NPCs in the world interact with you. The "Path of Resonance" questline will eventually force you to choose a side, so consider your playstyle before committing.

## Progression and Leveling Rewards

Exoria rewards consistent play through a tiered leveling system. Every five levels, players can claim free rewards from the menu, ranging from Credits to rare items.

| Level Milestone | Typical Rewards |
|---|---|
| **Level 5** | Basic Credits and XP Boost. |
| **Level 10** | Weapon Upgrade Materials and Rerolls. |
| **Level 15** | Special Skill Unlock (e.g., Special Cutscene Finisher). |
| **Level 25+** | Advanced Hoverboard parts and Rare Cosmetics. |

### Skill Tree Evolution
Skills in Exoria are not just unlocked by level; they often require a specific amount of Purity. For example, the **Earth Splitter** skill might require Level 35 and a neutral purity, whereas the **True Awakening** form depends heavily on your race and alignment. Each skill in the menu features a video preview, allowing you to see the move's range and impact before spending your points.

## World Activities: Mining, Hoverboards, and Cards

Beyond combat, there are several secondary systems that are vital for long-term success.

### Mining and Weapon Upgrades
To increase your damage output, you must upgrade your weapons using materials gathered through mining.
*   **Pickaxe**: Accessible via your inventory (usually bound to the '4' key).
*   **Mining Tiers**: As you mine more ores, you unlock higher tiers, allowing you to gather rarer "parts" needed for late-game sword enhancements.

### Hoverboard Customization
The world of Exoria is vast, and walking is inefficient. The Hoverboard (activated with the '3' key) is your primary mode of transport.
*   **Shop**: Visit the Hoverboard shop to buy new models or upgrade the speed of your current board.
*   **Upgrades**: Standard boards can be upgraded with Credits (Crowns) to significantly boost travel velocity.

### Card Collection
Progressing through the world allows you to collect "Exoria Cards." These are categorized into Combat Mastery, Crafting, and Gathering. Completing a full category set grants massive rewards, such as 2,000 XP and bonus Credits. Cards are typically earned through dungeons, quests, or as rare drops from stronger demons in areas like Whisper Stone Hollow.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Stronger demons in caves like Whisper Stone Hollow have significantly higher HP (200+) and use abilities like teleportation. Do not engage them without equipping your unlocked skills in the "Equip" menu.

## Advanced Challenges: Dungeons and Bosses

Once you have mastered the basics, you can test your skills against the game's toughest content.

1.  **The Demon Dimension**: A portal that opens every 30 minutes (e.g., 12:30, 1:00). Players must enter and defeat waves of enemies before the portal collapses to earn high-tier loot.
2.  **Marvel the Guardian Demon**: Located beneath Exorus Cave, this boss was once a protector but has since turned. Defeating Marvel is the primary way to unlock legendary weapons and exclusive cosmetics.
3.  **Cursed Objects**: Throughout the forest, you will find objects radiating dark energy. Removing these curses provides a steady stream of XP and contributes to your Purity rating.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock new skills in Exoria?**

A: Skills are unlocked via the Skill Tree menu. You must meet the minimum level requirement and often a specific Purity threshold (Positive or Negative). Once unlocked, you must manually "Equip" the skill to a slot (Z, X, T, or V) to use it in combat.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up in exoria roblox early access?**

A: The fastest way to level is by stacking Main Quests with Daily and Weekly objectives. Additionally, purifying Lost Souls in the forest provides a consistent XP stream that scales with the rarity of the soul (Common, Uncommon, Rare).

**Q: Can I change my race after starting the game?**

A: Yes, you can use Race Rerolls found in the menu. These are obtained via quest rewards, random spirit encounters, or by purchasing them with Robux.

**Q: How do I upgrade my hoverboard speed?**

A: Navigate to the Hoverboard Shop in the main hub. From there, you can spend Credits (earned from quests and combat) to upgrade the speed stat of your equipped board. Higher-tier boards like the "Blazed Board" have higher base speeds but cost more Credits.
