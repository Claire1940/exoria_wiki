## The Magic System: Casting Against Demons

One of the most unique aspects of Exoria is its "Input Magic" system. You don't simply click a button to cast a spell; you must memorize specific key sequences. To unlock the ability to cast magic, you must engage in combat—even simple punching will eventually trigger your magical awakening.

Once you have unlocked magic, you must invest at least **15 points into Intelligence** to access basic utility spells like healing. This is crucial when facing "Demon Type" bosses that deal high area-of-effect damage.

### Essential Magic Combos

| Spell Name | Key Combo | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fireball** | E -> R -> C | Launches a projectile that explodes on impact. |
| **Bello Ball** | C -> E -> R | A concentrated energy blast for single targets. |
| **Heal** | B -> E -> R -> B | Restores a portion of your health over time. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Magic casting leaves you vulnerable for a split second. Always ensure you have enough distance from Orcs or Pirates before starting a combo.

## Enemy Types and Scaling

As you explore the map, you will encounter various NPCs. While the "Demon" classification often applies to the higher-tier boss entities, the journey starts with more mundane threats that scale in difficulty.

### The Starter Mobs
*   **Hogs:** Surprisingly dangerous for beginners. They deal significant "fat" damage and should be approached with caution until you have a basic weapon.
*   **Weak Humans:** These look strong but are actually the best targets for early gold farming. Time your M1 attacks to break their combos.

### The Quest Line Entities
Once you have 15 Gold, head to the **Guild Hall** in the Castle to register. This unlocks the questing system, which is the primary way to earn Souls (used for buying class skills).

1.  **Orcs:** The first major hurdle. They are found in the wilderness and are perfect for practicing your magic combos.
2.  **Pirates:** Faster and more aggressive than Orcs. They often travel in groups near the coastal regions.
3.  **Pirate Captain (Boss):** A high-health target that requires dodging and consistent DPS.
4.  **The Golemite (Upcoming):** While mentioned in the quest logs, this entity is currently slated for a later 2026 update.

## Stat Optimization and Crystals

Progression in Exoria is tied to your level and the consumption of rare crystals. Every time you level up, you receive **10 Stat Points**. However, you can supplement your growth by finding Intelligence and Dexterity crystals hidden throughout the world or dropped by mobs.

*   **Intelligence Crystals:** Grants +5 to your Intelligence stat.
*   **Dexterity Crystals:** Grants +5 to your Dexterity stat.

There is a cap on how many crystals you can consume per stat, so use them as soon as you find them to maximize your early-game efficiency. For most "Demon Hunter" builds, we recommend a heavy investment in **Vitality** to survive the boss dungeons, combined with your primary damage stat (Strength or Dexterity).

## Boss Dungeons and Multiplayer Scaling

The most challenging demon types are found in the Boss Dungeons. A unique mechanic in Exoria is that boss health scales based on the number of players present. 

*   **Solo Play:** The boss has 100% base HP.
*   **Group Play (4+ Players):** The boss health may double or triple to maintain the challenge.

While it might seem easier to solo, bringing a group allows for "aggro swapping," where one player draws the boss's attention while others heal or charge their magic combos. This is the most effective way to farm the 1500+ Souls required for high-level class skills.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the exoria demon types guide for specific boss drops?**

A: Currently, boss drops like crystals and rare weapons are randomized. Most "Demonic" tier bosses have a higher chance of dropping Intelligence and Dexterity crystals compared to standard Orcs or Pirates.

**Q: How do I get more Souls for my class skills?**

A: The most efficient way to earn Souls is by completing the Guild Hall quests. Each quest completion grants a massive Soul bonus. Additionally, farming high-level NPCs like the Pirate Captain provides a steady stream of Souls.

**Q: What is the best stat for a beginner?**

A: We recommend putting 15 points into Intelligence first. This allows you to use the "Heal" magic combo (B-E-R-B), which significantly increases your survivability while farming mobs.

**Q: Can I change my class later in the game?**

A: Yes, you can switch classes by visiting a different weapon trainer and purchasing their specific weapon. However, remember that your Soul investment in skills is usually tied to that specific class, so switching late-game can be expensive.
