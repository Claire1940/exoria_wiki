The most critical discovery in Exoria's leveling system is that experience points are not distributed equally among party members. Instead, the game utilizes a damage-tracking system where the amount of XP you receive is directly proportional to the total damage you deal to a boss. This is why soloing is the gold standard for those wondering **exoria roblox how to level fast**.

When you fight in a large group, the boss's health pool is depleted quickly by multiple players. While the fight ends faster, your individual contribution—and thus your XP reward—is diluted. For instance, a level 31 player might only receive 1% to 2% XP when fighting the final boss in a full group, but that same player can earn up to 16% or 18% XP by soloing the same encounter.

## Optimizing Your Environment

Before engaging a boss, you should adjust your game settings to ensure no interruptions. In Exoria, other players can often disrupt your flow, especially if PVP (Player vs. Player) is enabled. If you are serious about **exoria roblox how to level fast**, you should switch your mode to PVE (Player vs. Environment).

Turning off PVP prevents other players from accidentally (or intentionally) hitting you, which can cancel your spell animations. In Exoria, if you are hit by a mob or player while casting a heal like VERV, the spell is often canceled, leaving you vulnerable. By focusing purely on PVE, you can maintain a consistent rotation of damage and healing.

## FAQ

**Q: Why am I getting so little XP when fighting bosses with my friends?**

A: XP in Exoria is largely based on the percentage of total damage you deal to the target. If you are in a large group, your individual damage contribution is lower, resulting in a smaller XP reward. To level faster, try to solo the boss or fight in a much smaller group.

**Q: What is the best stat to prioritize for a solo leveling build?**

A: Intelligence is the most important stat initially, as it unlocks the VERV and VERDE healing spells. Once you have enough Intelligence to cast these spells (30-70), you should invest in Faith to increase the amount of health restored per cast.

**Q: Are Golems worth farming for XP in the late game?**

A: No. At higher levels (30+), Golems and other common mobs provide less than 1% XP per kill. Soloing a boss can provide upwards of 16% XP, making it the only viable method for **exoria roblox how to level fast** once you reach the mid-to-late game.

**Q: Can I use the VERDE spell to level up my friends?**

A: Yes. If you are a high-level player, you can use the VERDE spell to keep your lower-level friends alive while they deal damage to a boss. Since they are doing the damage, they will receive the bulk of the XP, which is an excellent way to power-level new players.
