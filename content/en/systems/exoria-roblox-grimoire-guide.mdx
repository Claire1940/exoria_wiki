Once inside the tower, you must interact with the Grimoire Dealer. The acquisition process is a gacha-style system where players "spin" for a random book. You can use in-game currency (Yen) or Robux to purchase spins.

## Optimizing Your Stat Build

A common mistake highlighted in this **exoria roblox grimoire guide** is the improper allocation of stat points. Your build must synergize with your grimoire type. Most players receive three stat points per level, and how you invest them determines your effectiveness in PVP and PVE.

### Magic-Based Builds (Wind, Dark, Water)
If your grimoire relies on spells, you must balance your points between **Magic** and **Mana**. Without a deep Mana pool, you will find yourself unable to cast your most powerful abilities during long boss fights.
*   **Magic:** Increases the damage of your spells.
*   **Mana:** Increases the number of times you can cast before needing to recharge.

### Physical/Anti-Magic Builds
The Anti-Magic grimoire is unique because it ignores traditional magic stats. If you are lucky enough to pull this 0.1% rarity item, follow this build priority:
1.  **Strength:** This is your primary damage modifier. Unlocking the second move usually requires 100 points, while the final transformation requires 500.
2.  **Defense:** Crucial for surviving high-level wizards who can one-shot glass cannon builds. Aim for at least 100-150 points by level 200.
3.  **Mana/Magic:** Keep these at zero. They provide no benefit to Anti-Magic users.

## Unlocking Advanced Abilities

As you level up, your grimoire will "awaken," granting access to more complex moves. For physical users, this often includes mobility skills.

1.  **Anti-Magic Slash:** A basic forward projectile that can one-shot early mobs.
2.  **Anti-Magic Hurricane:** A spinning AoE (Area of Effect) attack that allows you to move while dealing damage.
3.  **Anti-Magic Liberation:** A long-range sword drop that deals massive burst damage.
4.  **Transformation:** The ultimate move (unlocked around Level 200 with 500 Strength). This grants wings, a flight buff, and new moves like Anti-Magic Burst.

### The Art of Jumping and Flying
Mobility is just as important as damage. In the desert region, you can find an NPC behind a house who offers the "Art of Jumping" for 10,000 Yen. Completing his quest—which involves double-jumping 100 times—will awaken your jump ability. However, if your grimoire allows for a "Flying Sword" or "Magic Flight" (typically mapped to the **F key**), the jump upgrade may be less of a priority.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best grimoire in Exoria for 2026?**

A: While "best" depends on playstyle, Anti-Magic is widely considered the most powerful due to its ability to negate enemy spells and its massive physical damage scaling. However, Blade Magic and Dark Magic are excellent alternatives for those who prefer traditional spellcasting.

**Q: How do I reset my stats if I picked the wrong build?**

A: Stat reset items can occasionally be obtained through codes or purchased in the in-game shop using Robux. It is highly recommended to follow an **exoria roblox grimoire guide** build early on to avoid the need for resets.

**Q: Where can I find the Grimoire Tower?**

A: The tower is a large, grey stone structure located several hundred meters from the starting village. If you see a large skull icon on your horizon or a tall spire reaching the clouds, you are heading in the right direction.

**Q: Can I trade grimoires with other players?**

A: Currently, grimoires are bound to the player's soul upon spinning. You cannot trade the books themselves, but you can trade items like necklaces and bracelets that provide stat boosts to your magic or health.
