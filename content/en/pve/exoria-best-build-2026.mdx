## Choosing Your Race: Demon vs. Non-Demon

One of the most significant factors in your **exoria best build 2026** is your choice of race. The game categorizes transformations and ultimate abilities based on whether your character belongs to a Demon or Non-Demon race. Each path offers a unique "transformation cut," which is a cinematic power-up that temporarily enhances your combat capabilities.

### Non-Demon Racial Path
Non-demon races (such as Humans or Celestials) focus on purity and speed. Their transformations often involve light-based visual effects and provide massive buffs to Agility and Attack Speed. If you prefer a "glass cannon" playstyle where you dodge every hit while dealing a thousand cuts, the non-demon path is likely your best choice for a sword build.

### Demon Racial Path
Demon races offer a more brutal approach to combat. Their transformations are characterized by dark energy and provide buffs to raw Strength and Lifesteal. This path is ideal for players who want to sustain themselves in the middle of a mob of enemies without needing constant healing items.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the exoria best build 2026 for beginners?**

A: For beginners, a Non-Demon Human build focusing on Strength and Stamina is recommended. It is the most balanced path and allows you to learn the parry timings without the high-risk mechanics of the Demon race.

**Q: Can I switch from a Demon race to a Non-Demon race later?**

A: Yes, but it requires a "Soul Purge" item, which is rare or available through the premium shop. It is better to decide on your racial path early to avoid wasting stat reset tokens.

**Q: Is the Upgraded Sword Class better than the Magic Class in 2026?**

A: In the current meta, the Upgraded Sword Class has higher single-target DPS, making it superior for boss hunting. However, Magic Classes still excel at large-scale mob farming and area control.

**Q: How do I unlock the new transformation cutscenes?**

A: Transformation cutscenes are unlocked by completing your Race's "Mastery Trial" once you reach level 60 and have the Upgraded Class equipped. Each race has a unique trial located in their respective home territory.
