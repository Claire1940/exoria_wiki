## The Ether Merchant and Exclusive Accessories

Once you have successfully farmed Ether Relics from the Train Event, your next stop should be the Merchant. This NPC is the only source for exclusive accessories that cannot be obtained through standard dungeon drops or boss farming.

For players focusing on a specific demon type, these accessories are transformative. They often provide percentage-based buffs to your primary stats, making them far more valuable than standard gear. Additionally, the Merchant allows you to sell unwanted items from other dungeons, such as the Boss Dungeon, providing a reliable way to clear your inventory and earn extra currency.

### Recommended Merchant Purchases
*   **Ether-Infused Cloak:** Provides a 10% boost to all elemental damage, perfect for Infernal types.
*   **Void Pendant:** Increases mana pool by 20%, a must-have for Void demon types.
*   **Relic of Fortitude:** Enhances life-steal effects for Blood demons.

> **Warning:** The Merchant's stock can be limited. Always prioritize accessories that complement your current demon type's passive abilities before spending relics on general items.
