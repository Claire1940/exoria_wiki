## S-Tier: The Dungeon Masters

### Spear Class
The Spear remains the undisputed king of the **exoria roblox dungeon tier list** because of its ability to "poke" enemies from outside their hitboxes. In high-level dungeons where bosses like the Ancient Golem can one-shot players, maintaining distance is the best defensive strategy. 

To find the Spear Trainer, you must travel past the Golem fields. Follow the path leading out of the main city, take a left at the fork, and continue until you see the stone Golems. Avoid their heavy attacks, climb the wooden ladders nearby, and you will find the trainer overlooking the valley.

### Fist (Akuma) Class
The Fist class, often referred to as the Akuma style, excels in dungeons that require high mobility. Its ability to weave in and out of combat makes it perfect for dodging telegraphed AOE attacks. 

The trainer is hidden deep within the Goblin territory. You will need to navigate through the Goblin camp, cross a wooden bridge over a waterfall, and locate a small hut. This class is highly recommended for players who enjoy a fast-paced, "brawler" style of gameplay.

## A-Tier: Reliable Powerhouses

### Sword Class
The Sword is the most accessible class in the game and remains a staple in any dungeon party. It offers a perfect 50/50 split between offensive pressure and defensive utility. Because it is located right at the start of the game in the Falaraga church, it is the most common class for early-game progression. While it doesn't have the extreme range of the Spear, its skill set is versatile enough to handle almost any encounter.

### Hammer Class
If your goal is to clear "trash mobs" (the smaller enemies that swarm players in hallways), the Hammer is your best friend. Its heavy attacks have built-in knockback and stun mechanics. In the 2026 meta, the Hammer is essential for keeping enemies grouped up for Spear or Fist users to finish off. You can find the Hammer trainer inside the Guild Hall recruitment center in the main city.

## B-Tier: The Niche Specialists

### Dagger Class
The Dagger class sits at the bottom of our **exoria roblox dungeon tier list** not because it is weak, but because it is difficult to use effectively in a dungeon setting. Daggers require the player to be extremely close to the enemy, which is dangerous against bosses with "stomp" or "spin" mechanics. However, for players who focus on PVP or hunting specific single-target mini-bosses, the Dagger's critical hit rate is unmatched. The trainer is located behind the crates near the Tavern/Blue Bay area.

## How to Optimize Your Dungeon Build

Once you have selected a high-tier class from the **exoria roblox dungeon tier list**, you need to focus on your stat distribution. Exoria uses a traditional RPG system where your weapon damage scales with specific attributes.

1.  **Strength-Heavy (Hammer/Sword):** Focus on raw power to break enemy guards and increase stun duration.
2.  **Dexterity-Heavy (Spear/Dagger):** Prioritize attack speed and critical hit chance. This is vital for the Spear to maintain its "poke" distance.
3.  **Agility-Heavy (Fist):** Focus on stamina regeneration and movement speed to ensure you never get caught in a boss's AOE.

## Dungeon Strategy by Class

### Solo Play
If you are running dungeons alone, the **Spear** is the safest bet. Use your range to pull one enemy at a time. If you get surrounded, use your knockback skill to reset the fight. The **Sword** is also viable for solo play due to its balanced defensive options.

### Group Play
In a group of four, the ideal composition includes one **Hammer** to tank and crowd control, two **Spears** or **Fists** for primary damage, and a **Sword** user to fill the gaps. The Hammer should always engage first to draw "aggro" (enemy attention), allowing the higher DPS classes to attack from the sides or rear.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my class after talking to a trainer?**

A: Yes, but it usually requires a specific item or a gold fee to reset your current mastery. It is highly recommended to test the basic versions of weapons before committing to a specific trainer's skill tree.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm gold for trainers?**

A: The Goblin camp near the Fist trainer is excellent for early-to-mid-game gold farming. The Goblins drop coins frequently and are relatively easy to defeat if you use the AOE skills from the Hammer or Sword classes.

**Q: Does this exoria roblox dungeon tier list apply to PVP?**

A: Not necessarily. In PVP, the Dagger and Fist classes often move up to S-Tier because of their high burst damage and mobility. Dungeons favor reach and AOE, whereas PVP favors speed and surprise.

**Q: What is the most difficult trainer to reach in Exoria?**

A: The Fist (Akuma) trainer is widely considered the hardest to find because the path through the Goblin camp is a maze, and the bridge near the waterfall is easy to miss if you aren't looking for the specific opening in the rocks.
