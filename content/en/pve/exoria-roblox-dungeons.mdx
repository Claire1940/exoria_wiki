## Getting Started: The First Steps to Power

When you first spawn into Exoria, you will find yourself on a beach. Your immediate priority is not combat, but exploration and economy. Before you can tackle any **exoria roblox dungeons**, you need to establish a baseline of gold and equipment. Follow the trail leading away from the beach toward the Market.

The Market is the central hub for all players. Here, you will find the Trinket Seller, class trainers, and the registration NPC. Trinkets are small items scattered across the map that can be sold for gold. This is the fastest way for a new player to accumulate the 15 gold required for the first major quest registration.

| NPC / Location | Purpose | Key Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **Trinket Seller** | Exchange found items for Gold | None |
| **Hammer Trainer** | Unlocks the Hammer class and weapon | Gold / Souls |
| **Registration NPC** | Unlocks the main questline (Orcs/Pirates) | **15 Gold** |
| **Akuma Trainer** | Unlocks Fist-based combat (near spawn) | **2,500 Souls** |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not rush into combat with Golems early on. Even at level 26, players can be "folded" by Golems. Stick to Goblins, Orcs, or Boars until you have unlocked your first magic spells.

## Unlocking Magic and Spellcasting

Magic is the backbone of any successful run through the **exoria roblox dungeons**. To even begin your journey as a mage, you must invest at least **15 points into Intelligence (INT)**. Increasing your Intelligence not only unlocks the ability to cast but also improves your casting efficiency, mana pool size, and spell speed.

The magic system in Exoria uses a "key-string" casting method similar to classic elemental RPGs. You must input specific keys to manifest a spell.

| Spell Name | Key Combo | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Velo Ball** | C - E - R | A utility ball that boosts speed and mobility. |
| **Fireball** | E - R - C | Primary offensive projectile for high DPS. |
| **Self Heal** | (Varies) | Essential for solo dungeon runs. |
| **Team Heal** | (Varies) | Vital for group boss raids. |

## Combat Mechanics: The Chain-Combo System

Exoria utilizes a combat system inspired by high-action titles like *Black Desert Online*. Rather than simply clicking to attack, you must chain together M1 (Left Click), M2 (Right Click), and Shift modifiers to perform advanced maneuvers.

1.  **Basic Barrage:** Holding Shift + Right Click often triggers a barrage or kick combo.
2.  **Sweeps:** Shift + Left Click is typically used for area-of-effect sweeps to manage multiple mobs.
3.  **Weaving:** If you time your block or dodge perfectly as an NPC swings, your character will perform a "weave" animation, granting you a window for a counter-attack.
4.  **Skills & Souls:** Every skill you learn from a trainer requires **Souls**. Souls are gathered by defeating enemies. While most classes require around 1,000 to 1,500 souls per skill, the Akuma (Fist) class is the most expensive, requiring 2,500 souls per upgrade because it does not require a purchased weapon.

## Navigating Exoria Roblox Dungeons

Once you have reached level 15 and secured a reliable healing spell, you are ready to enter the dungeon areas. The entrance is located near the marketplace; instead of turning toward the orc fortress, continue straight and locate the lever-operated platform.

The **exoria roblox dungeons** are notoriously dark and dangerous. The environment changes significantly once you descend, with the music shifting to a more somber tone and the lighting becoming extremely limited.

### Dungeon Preparation Checklist
*   **Level 15+:** Attempting these areas earlier will likely result in a loss of lives.
*   **Velo Spell:** The speed boost from Velo is necessary to dodge the heavy-hitting boss telegraphs.
*   **Crystals:** Throughout the dungeon, mobs will drop Crystals. **Eat these immediately.** For example, an Intelligence Crystal will provide a permanent (asterisk) stat boost to your magic capabilities.
*   **Group Up:** While the first boss raid (the "Weak Boss") can be soloed by skilled players, the second boss raid is often glitched or significantly harder, requiring a full party.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Exoria features a limited life system. If you lose all your lives to mobs or environmental hazards, the consequences are severe. Always ensure your "Self Heal" is ready before engaging a mob group in the dungeon.

## Advanced Progression: The Pirate Quest & Beyond

After completing the initial Orc quest and the first Boss Raid, return to the registration NPC in town. He will provide the "Pirate Quest." This takes you to a new coastal region where the enemies have higher health pools and faster attack patterns.

The Pirate Captain boss spawns every three minutes in this general location. He is a "stat-check" boss—if you haven't been consuming the crystals dropped by mobs to boost your base stats, his high damage output will quickly deplete your lives.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock magic in Exoria?**

A: You must first put 15 points into your Intelligence (INT) stat. Once you have the required stats, you can cast spells by inputting specific key combinations like C-E-R for the Velo ball or E-R-C for Fireball.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm gold for the 15 gold registration fee?**

A: The most efficient way is to roam the beach and market areas looking for Trinkets. These items spawn on the ground and can be sold to the Trinket Seller in the market. Alternatively, farming Goblins or Orcs has a chance to drop gold directly.

**Q: What should I do if the boss raid in the exoria roblox dungeons is glitched?**

A: If the boss is not taking damage or the queue is not working, it is recommended to server-hop. Use the "Rumors" or "Request" dialogue with the NPC to ensure your quest is active before entering the raid portal.

**Q: Why is the Akuma class more expensive than the Hammer class?**

A: The Akuma trainer requires 2,500 souls per skill because the class relies entirely on fist combat. Unlike the Hammer or Sword classes, you do not need to spend gold on weapon upgrades, so the soul cost is increased to balance the progression.
