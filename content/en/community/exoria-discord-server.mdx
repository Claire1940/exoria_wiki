## The Purity System and Progression

Exoria introduces a unique "Purity" mechanic that tracks your moral alignment based on your actions. This isn't just a cosmetic bar; it dictates which areas of the world react to you and which abilities you can unlock in the skill tree.

*   **Positive Purity (Orange):** Earned by purifying floating spirits and "gripping" demons. High purity can attract specific holy enemies but grants access to Light-based resonance skills.
*   **Negative Purity (Purple):** Earned by embracing darker powers or sparing certain entities. This path leads to shadow-based abilities and high-risk, high-reward combat modifiers.

You can monitor your status via the bar at the bottom of the UI. If you find yourself struggling to maintain a specific alignment, the **exoria discord server** has dedicated guides on the most efficient "Spirit Cleansing" routes to manipulate your purity levels quickly.

## Skill Trees and Resonance

Progression in Exoria is tied to both your level and your purity. Every five levels, you unlock a new reward, but your core abilities must be purchased through the Skill Tree menu. The tree is divided into three main categories: Combat Mastery, Crafting, and Gathering.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when spending your skill points. While you can reset your tree, the cost in credits increases significantly with each use.

### Essential Early-Game Skills
1.  **Violent Flurry (Z Skill):** Unleashes multiple rapid sword strikes. This is your primary tool for clearing groups of demons.
2.  **Earth Splitter (X Skill):** A powerful shockwave that knocks back enemies. Use this when you are surrounded or need to create space to heal.
3.  **Passive HP Regen:** Unlocked at level 45, this is vital for surviving the Demon Dimension dungeons.

## Exploration and Hoverboards

The world of Exoria is vast, ranging from the structured Exorcist Academy to the dangerous Whisper Stone Hollow. To make travel more efficient, every player is granted a default hoverboard. As you earn credits (CR) from quests, you can visit the board shop to upgrade your speed or purchase entirely new models.

For example, the **Blazed Board** costs 1,000 credits but offers a significant speed boost over the starter board. Upgrading your board's speed is often a better investment than buying new gear in the first ten levels, as it allows you to complete "Fetch Quests" much faster.

Players often share the best mining locations within the **exoria discord server**, as mining ore is required for high-level crafting recipes. Look for glowing nodes in caves; these often yield rare materials used to enhance your sword's base damage.
