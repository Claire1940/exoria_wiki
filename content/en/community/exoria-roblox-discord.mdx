## Progression and Purity Mechanics

Progression in Exoria is tied to both your Level and your Purity bar. The Purity bar is visible at the bottom of your screen: orange represents positive purity (Light), while purple represents negative purity (Shadow).

1.  **Leveling Up:** Earn XP by completing main quests, daily tasks, and defeating demons in the forest.
2.  **Skill Unlocks:** Skills are gated by both Level and Purity. For example, some high-tier Sword skills require Level 15 and at least 20 Purity points.
3.  **The Hoverboard:** To navigate the large map quickly, use the Hoverboard (Keybind: 3). You can upgrade its speed using Crowns (CR) at the specialized shop.
4.  **Mining:** Use your pickaxe (Keybind: 4) on ore veins to gather crafting materials. This is essential for mid-game gear upgrades.

> **⚠️ Warning:** High purity levels may attract specific aggressive enemies in certain zones. Always keep an eye on your surroundings when farming spirits in the forest.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the official Exoria Roblox Discord link?**

**Q: How do I get more Race Rerolls in Exoria?**
