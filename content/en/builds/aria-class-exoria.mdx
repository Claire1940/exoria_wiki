## The Purity System and Karma

A defining mechanic for every **aria class exoria** user is the Purity Bar. This system tracks your character's alignment based on the choices you make throughout the game's questlines. In Exoria, your path isn't set in stone; you can lean toward the path of a saint or a more demonic influence.

Your Purity level directly affects how your skills manifest. For an Aria, this might mean the difference between casting "Holy Light" or a "Demonic Burst." The quests you accept and the NPCs you assist will shift your Purity Bar, which in turn influences your transformation options later in the game.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Your choices are permanent for that character slot. A low Purity rating may lock you out of certain "Holy" upgrades, while high Purity might prevent you from accessing Demon-form transformations.

## Combat Mechanics: Dash Cancellation and Flow

The developers have emphasized that the combat system in Exoria is designed for "freedom and flow." For the **aria class exoria**, this means you are no longer a "glass cannon" that is easy to catch. 

By mastering dash cancellation, you can:
*   **Bait Attacks:** Start a long-winded chant to force an opponent to dodge, then cancel into a dash to reposition.
*   **Combo Extension:** Use a dash to close the gap after a knockback spell, allowing for follow-up attacks.
*   **Survival:** Cancel out of a spell if you see a boss telegraphing a massive AoE attack.

> **💡 Tip:** Practice your dash timing in the training grounds. The window for cancellation is generous, but doing it too early will waste your mana without triggering the spell.

## Combat Strategy for Aria Players

Playing the **aria class exoria** requires a different mindset than the Sword class. You must be aware of your surroundings at all times. Because your damage is often delayed through chants, predicting enemy movement is key.

*   **Keep Distance:** Use your dash to maintain a safe gap.
*   **Manage Mana:** Don't spam your highest-cost spells; use low-cost chants to chip away at health.
*   **Utilize the Environment:** Many Aria spells have AoE effects that can be used to trap enemies in corners or narrow corridors.

The fluidity of the combat system ensures that even if an enemy gets close, a well-timed dash cancellation can save your life. This makes the Aria a formidable opponent in both PvE and PvP scenarios.
