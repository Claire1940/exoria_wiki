## Advanced Combat: Dash Cancellation

To excel as an **exoria aria** user, you must master the new dash cancellation mechanic. In previous iterations of Roblox combat games, players were often "locked" into animations, making them vulnerable to counter-attacks. For a mage, this is a game-changer. It allows you to start casting a powerful spell, and if you see a demon closing the gap, you can dash away instantly to reposition. This creates a sense of "flow" that makes the combat feel incredibly smooth and responsive.

1.  **Initiate Skill:** Start your Aria incantation.
2.  **Observe Enemy:** Watch for incoming projectiles or lunges.
3.  **Dash Cancel:** Input the dash command during the wind-up frames.
4.  **Reposition:** Use the momentum to slide into a safer casting zone.
