### Mastering the Flow
By mastering the timing of dash cancellation, an Aria can:
1.  **Bait Attacks:** Start a cast to draw a demon in, then dash away instantly.
2.  **Reposition Spells:** Adjust the angle of a projectile mid-combat.
3.  **Combo Extension:** Use dashes to bridge the gap between two different spell animations.

This system ensures that the combat feels smooth and responsive. It moves away from the "static" combat found in older Roblox titles and toward a more modern, action-oriented experience.
