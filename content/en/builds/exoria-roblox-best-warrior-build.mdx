## Stat Allocation and Priority

To make the most of your sword skills, your stat points must be distributed efficiently. A common mistake among new players is spreading points too thinly across all categories. For a dedicated warrior, focus on Strength (STR) and Agility (AGI).

*   **Strength (STR):** This is your primary damage stat. It increases the scaling of all sword-based abilities.
*   **Agility (AGI):** Essential for closing the gap and dodging enemy projectiles. It also reduces the wind-up time for certain heavy attacks.
*   **Vitality (VIT):** While not the primary focus, having enough health to survive a single combo is mandatory for any **exoria roblox best warrior build**.

| Level Range | STR Priority | AGI Priority | VIT Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Levels 1-20** | 70% | 20% | 10% |
| **Levels 21-50** | 60% | 30% | 10% |
| **Levels 51+** | 50% | 40% | 10% |

> **Warning:** Neglecting Agility will make you a "sitting duck" in high-level PvP. Even the strongest sword strike is useless if it never connects.

## Advanced Combat Strategies

To truly excel with the **exoria roblox best warrior build**, you must master the art of the transformation. Transformation cuts are not just for show; they provide a brief window of invincibility and a massive stat boost.

*   **Transformation Timing:** Save your transformation for when your health is below 40% or when the boss enters its final phase. The burst of power can often turn the tide of a losing battle.
*   **Animation Canceling:** Some upgraded sword skills allow you to cancel the recovery frames by jumping or dashing. This allows for faster combos and better reactive play.
*   **Positioning:** As a warrior, you should always aim to be behind the enemy. This minimizes the risk of taking direct damage while allowing you to unleash your full combo.

> **💡 Tip:** Practice your transformation timing in the training grounds. Knowing exactly how long the animation lasts will prevent you from being caught off guard in real combat.
