*   **Utility Accessories:** Reduce cooldowns on your most powerful abilities.

## Farming and Progression Guide

To reach the endgame, you must follow a strict progression path that utilizes the new questing system introduced in 2026. The addition of two main quests, one daily quest, and one weekly quest provides a steady stream of XP that was previously missing.

1.  **Complete Daily Quests First:** These provide a significant XP boost and are often simple tasks like clearing a specific number of enemies.
2.  **Participate in Every Train Event:** Even if you cannot clear all three waves, the XP and coins from the first two waves are essential for early-game growth.
3.  **Sell Excess Loot:** Use the Merchant to sell items you've gathered from Boss Dungeons that don't fit your current build. This is the fastest way to accumulate coins for gear upgrades.
4.  **Stack Your Items:** With the new stackable item update, you can now carry more potions and buff materials, allowing for longer farming sessions without returning to town.

> **Warning:** Do not ignore your weekly quests. The rewards for these often include rare materials required for high-level crafting that cannot be found elsewhere.

## FAQ

**Q: Which of the exoria roblox classes is best for beginners?**

A: The Vanguard (Tank) is widely considered the best for beginners. It offers the highest room for error due to its large health pool and defensive capabilities, making it easier to learn the mechanics of the Train Event and Boss Dungeons.

**Q: How do I get Ether Relics for my class accessories?**

A: Ether Relics are primarily obtained by completing the Train Event, which occurs every 30 minutes. You must clear the waves of enemies to earn them. These relics can then be traded at the Merchant for exclusive class-enhancing accessories.

**Q: Can I change my class or build later in the game?**

A: Yes, you can pivot your build by changing your equipped weapons and accessories. However, because specialized accessories from the Merchant cost Ether Relics, it is recommended to decide on a primary role early to avoid wasting resources.

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up in 2026?**

A: The fastest way to level up is to combine the new Main, Daily, and Weekly quests with consistent participation in the Train Event. The event provides "loads of XP" according to the latest update logs, especially if you can clear all three waves.
