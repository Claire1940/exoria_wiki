### Strategy for Ether Relic Farming
To maximize your efficiency during the Train Event, follow these steps:
*   **Positioning:** Stand on the elevated platforms near the train tracks. This allows you to rain down arrows without being swarmed by ground-based mobs.
*   **Skill Rotation:** Use your AOE (Area of Effect) skills during Wave 1 and 2 to clear the fodder quickly. Save your high-damage single-target skills for the Wave 3 mini-boss.
*   **Currency Management:** Don't spend your Ether Relics immediately. Save them for the "Etheric Sight" goggles, which are considered BiS (Best-in-Slot) for the **exoria roblox best archer build**.

Your skill tree should complement your gear. In 2026, the meta has shifted away from purely passive builds toward active skill management.
