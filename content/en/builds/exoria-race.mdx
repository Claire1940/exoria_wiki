## Understanding the Exoria Race System

The core of Exoria’s character progression begins at the customization screen. Unlike many other Roblox RPGs where race is purely aesthetic, your **exoria race** dictates which transformation cutscenes you see and which skill variants you can equip. Currently, the game distinguishes between two primary lineages: Humans (often categorized as non-demon races) and Demons.

While Humans are the most common result when starting a new save, they possess unique advantages in the "Sword Class" and "Exorcist" paths. Conversely, the Demon race offers high-risk, high-reward gameplay with unique "Demon Forms" that provide massive stat boosts at the cost of different progression requirements.

## Progression Paths and the Purity System

Exoria features a unique "Purity System" that tracks how you interact with the world. This system interacts differently with each **exoria race**. Humans gain bonus EXP and Purity points by completing purification tasks—cleansing areas of demonic influence. 

Demons, on the other hand, often have to manage a "Hunger" or "Corruption" gauge. While they can still complete quests, their progression is often tied to defeating powerful enemies and "consuming" their essence, leading to different cutscenes and narrative outcomes.

### Leveling Strategy for 2026

1.  **The Academy Phase (Levels 1-20):** Regardless of race, head to the Academy immediately. Pick up the Hoverboard to expedite travel between quest markers.
2.  **The Purification Phase (Levels 20-50):** Focus on the "Purify" areas found throughout the map. These provide massive bonus EXP for Humans and help balance the corruption for Demon players.
3.  **The Class Upgrade (Level 50+):** Once you hit the level cap for the base class, visit your respective trainer to unlock the upgraded sword or magic skills specific to your race.

## Combat Mechanics: Race-Specific Tactics

In PvP (Player vs. Player) scenarios, your **exoria race** defines your threat level. Humans typically excel in "Combo Breaking" and "Stun Locking" due to their precision-based sword skills. A Human player using the upgraded Sword Class can chain multiple fast attacks to prevent a Demon from activating their transformation.

Demons play a "Tank and Burst" game. They often take more damage initially but become nearly unstoppable once their unique transformation cutscene triggers. In 2026, the meta revolves around managing your transformation meter to ensure it activates during the most critical moments of a boss fight or duel.

### Racial Stat Bonuses (Estimated)

*   **Human:** +10% Movement Speed, +15% Purification Efficiency, +5% Sword Attack Speed.
*   **Demon:** +20% Raw Damage, +10% Health Pool, unique "Leap" or "Dash" mechanics.
*   **Hybrid:** Balanced stats across the board with access to both "Light" and "Dark" skill trees.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my exoria race after I have started the game?**

A: Yes, you can reroll your race at any time using Race Spins in the customization menu. However, keep in mind that changing your race may reset some race-specific skill progress or require you to unlock new versions of your upgraded class skills.

**Q: Is the Demon race objectively better than the Human race?**

A: No. While Demons have higher raw damage and flashy transformations, Humans have better utility, faster attack speeds, and easier access to the Purity system's EXP bonuses. The "best" race depends entirely on whether you prefer a fast, technical playstyle (Human) or a heavy-hitting, transformation-based playstyle (Demon).

**Q: How do I get more Race Spins in 2026?**

A: You can earn spins by completing weekly "Elite" quests at the Academy, participating in limited-time world events, or by using codes released on the game's official social media channels. You can also purchase them with Robux if you want to skip the grind.

**Q: Do races affect the hoverboard or travel speed?**

A: Your race does not directly affect the speed of your hoverboard, but some races have passive movement speed buffs that apply when you are on foot, making certain races feel more "agile" during combat and exploration.

**Q: Are there more races coming to Exoria?**

A: The developers have hinted at "Ancient" lineages and more Hybrid variations in future 2026 updates. For now, focus on mastering the Human and Demon paths as they are the most fleshed-out systems in the current version of the game.
