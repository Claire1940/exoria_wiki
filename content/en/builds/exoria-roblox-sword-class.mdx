## Mastering the Combat System: Dash Cancellation

To truly excel with the **exoria roblox sword class**, you must master the dash cancellation mechanic. In many Roblox RPGs, you are locked into an animation once a skill is activated. Exoria breaks this mold by allowing players to cancel the recovery frames of their attacks with a well-timed dash.

This creates a "flow" state where a sword user can strike, dash to the side to avoid a counter-attack, and immediately resume their combo. It significantly raises the skill ceiling, especially in PvP encounters where being stationary for even a second can lead to a quick defeat.

### How to Execute a Dash Cancel
1. Initiate a basic M1 string or a specific sword skill.
2. Watch for the "Active Frames" (the moment the hit connects).
3. Immediately input your dash command (Shift or Button X).
4. Follow up with a secondary skill to bridge the gap.

> **💡 Tip:** Use dash cancellation to reposition behind bosses during their heavy attack windups. This allows the **exoria roblox sword class** to maintain constant DPS without taking damage.

## The Path of Purity: Good vs. Evil

One of the most discussed aspects of the **exoria roblox sword class** is how the Purity Bar affects your endgame build. Every quest you take in the world of Exoria has a moral weight. Choosing to help a citizen might increase your purity, while taking a more "mercenary" approach might decrease it.

For sword users, this choice is vital. High-purity builds often unlock skills that have "Holy" or "Light" properties, which are exceptionally effective against high-tier demon bosses. Low-purity builds, on the other hand, might focus on "Hellfire" or "Shadow" attributes, providing massive damage boosts at the cost of higher stamina consumption.

| Purity Level | Skill Attribute | Best For... |

## FAQ

**Q: How do I change my purity if I made the wrong choices?**

A: You can shift your purity by completing repeatable "Atonement" or "Corruption" quests found in the main hub city. These quests are specifically designed to help players realign their **exoria roblox sword class** build without starting a new character.

**Q: Is the sword class better than the Aria class?**

A: Neither is objectively "better." The sword class excels in solo play and aggressive melee combat, while the Aria class is superior for group play and AOE crowd control. Most high-level raids require a balance of both.

**Q: Can I use the sword class on mobile?**

A: Yes, Exoria is fully optimized for mobile, PC, and consoles. The UI for the sword class includes large, responsive buttons for dash cancellation and skill execution on touchscreens.

**Q: Does my race affect my base sword damage?**

A: Race primarily affects your transformation forms and certain stat scalings (like health or stamina), but the base damage of your **exoria roblox sword class** skills is largely determined by your weapon and purity level.
