## The Foundations of Fluid Storage: Pressure Pipes

When you reach the mid-game, standard buckets and small tanks no longer suffice. You need "Super Tanks"—massive, modular structures capable of holding millions of millibuckets of lava, acid, or honey. The Pressure Pipes mod is the gold standard for this. Unlike traditional boxy tanks, these are completely modular, allowing you to build any shape, including spheres.

To begin your fluid tank build, you will need a few core components. The Tank Controller is the brain of the operation, while Tank Walls and Tank Indicators form the physical structure.

### Essential Tank Components

| Component | Primary Function | Key Materials |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tank Controller** | Forms the multi-block and provides the GUI | Diamond, Redstone, Iron |
| **Tank Wall** | The main structural block; stores 32 buckets | Pipe Sealant, Iron Plates |
| **Tank Indicator** | Provides a visual level of the fluid stored | Glass Panes, Pipe Sealant |
| **Fluid Filter** | Restricts the tank to a specific liquid type | Iron Bars, Iron Plates |
| **Fluid Intake/Outlet** | Points for pumping liquids in or out | Fluid Interface, Iron |

> **💡 Tip:** Every Tank Wall or Indicator block added to the structure increases the total capacity by 32,000 mB (32 buckets). A well-constructed sphere can easily hold over 6 million mB.

## Designing a Spherical Tank Build

Building a sphere in Minecraft can be a geometric nightmare without the right tools. To achieve a professional, NASA-inspired spherical tank, you should utilize the **Enhanced Building Guide**. This tool projects a ghost image of the shape you want to build, allowing you to place blocks rapidly.

### Step-by-Step Construction

1.  **Place the Building Guide:** Set the guide in the center of your designated industrial area and provide a Redstone signal (like a lever).
2.  **Configure the Shape:** Right-click the guide to switch the mode to "Sphere." Adjust the radius (a 9x9x9 or 14x14x14 diameter is recommended for high capacity).
3.  **Select Your Blocks:** While early builds might use **Tyrian** blocks, they can look overly "busy." For a cleaner, industrial look in 2026, **Laboratory Blocks** or **Factory Blocks** are preferred for the outer shell.
4.  **Auto-Build:** Hold right-click with your blocks to fill the projected sphere. 
5.  **Integrate the Tech:** Replace specific blocks on the sphere's surface with your Tank Controller, Fluid Intake, and Tank Indicators.

## Automating Fluid Collection

A tank is useless if it's empty. In Exoria, lava and acid are your primary power and processing fluids. To fill your new build, you must set up a long-distance pumping system using **Ender Tanks** and **Waystones**.

### Pumping Strategy

| Tool | Role | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **BC Factory Pump** | Extracts liquid from the world | Place over large lava lakes in the Core or Underground. |
| **Ender Tank** | Cross-dimensional transport | Color-code your tanks (e.g., Red-White-White for Lava In). |
| **Waystone** | Fast travel to pump sites | Allows you to quickly move the pump once a lake is drained. |
| **Stirling/Wooden Engines** | Powers the pump | Simple Redstone-powered engines are sufficient for early automation. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always use a **Fluid Filter** on your Tank Controller. This prevents accidental contamination if you hook up the wrong Ender Tank line to your main storage.

## The Survival "Tank": Armor and Flexible Tools

Surviving the Underground dimension requires more than just high-capacity storage; it requires a character build that can withstand mobs and environmental hazards. This part of the **exoria tank build guide** covers the "Flexible" toolset—essential gear that bridges the gap between early-game iron and late-game alloys.

### The Flexible Pickaxe
The Flexible Pickaxe is a standout tool in Exoria. It boasts Diamond-level mining speed and high durability, but its real value is its repairability and versatility.

**Crafting Requirements:**
*   **Tungsten Plates:** Smelted from Tungsten dust.
*   **Obsidian Rods:** Crafted from Burnium and Obsidian dust.
*   **Slime Block:** Can be created using a Botania Alchemy Catalyst (Cactus to Slimeballs).
*   **Diamond Pickaxe:** Used as a base component for the flexible upgrade.

### Armor Progression for Tanks
If you are struggling with durability, consider **Chainmail Armor**. While it has slightly less protection than Iron, it is much cheaper to maintain using Iron Nuggets and provides a "snazzy" aesthetic while you save resources for Tier 2 and Tier 3 plating.

| Gear Piece | Attack/Defense | Special Property |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flexible Pickaxe** | 7 Attack Damage | Mines almost everything at Diamond speed. |
| **Chainmail Set** | Balanced Defense | High durability-to-cost ratio for early exploration. |
| **Mana Tablet** | Utility | Carry Botania mana to power various trinkets and tools. |

## Resource Automation: The Igneous Extruder

To maintain a massive base build, you need a constant supply of stone. Instead of manual mining, integrate an **Igneous Extruder** into your fluid system. By providing it with a bucket of lava and an infinite water source (via the **Aqueous Accumulator**), you can generate infinite Smooth Stone, Basalt, or Obsidian.

1.  **Craft the Machine Frame:** Requires Tin and Iron Plates.
2.  **Aqueous Accumulator:** Place this in a 3x3 of water to generate infinite water.
3.  **Input Lava:** Use a **Powered Tank Fluid Outlet** from your main spherical tank to pipe lava directly into the Extruder.
4.  **Output to Storage:** Connect the Extruder to an Ender Chest or a Drawer system to keep your building supplies stocked.

## FAQ

**Q: Why should I build a spherical tank instead of a standard square one?**

A: While square tanks are easier to build, spherical tanks utilize the modularity of the Pressure Pipes mod to create a more compact and aesthetically pleasing industrial look. Additionally, using the Enhanced Building Guide makes the process nearly as fast as building a box.

**Q: How do I repair the Flexible Pickaxe in this exoria tank build guide?**

A: Flexible tools can be repaired using the primary material they are made of (Tungsten) or via specialized repair stations once you progress further into the tech tree. They are designed to be long-lasting alternatives to standard vanilla tools.

**Q: Can I store multiple liquids in one modular tank?**

A: No. Each multi-block tank formed by a Tank Controller can only hold one type of fluid. You should build separate spheres for Lava, Acid, and Water to keep your resource lines clean and organized.

**Q: What is the best way to move fluids between dimensions?**

A: Ender Tanks are the most reliable method. By color-coding the wool pads on top, you can create dedicated channels for different fluids that work across the Overworld, the Underground, and the Core.
