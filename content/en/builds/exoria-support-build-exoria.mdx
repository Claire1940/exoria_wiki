## Core Stats for the Exoria Support Build Exoria

The foundation of any effective support character lies in the stat points earned during leveling. In Exoria, you receive 10 stat points per level, and how you allocate these determines your effectiveness in combat. For a support-focused role, your primary focus should be Intelligence (INT) and Vitality (VIT).

Intelligence is the most critical stat because it unlocks the fundamental healing move. You need a minimum of **15 Intelligence** to access basic restorative magic. Beyond this, Vitality ensures you have the health pool to survive stray hits from bosses while you are busy channeling spells for your teammates.

| Stat Name | Priority | Recommended Value | Effect on Support Build |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Intelligence** | **High** | 50+ | Increases spell potency and unlocks the Healing Move. |
| **Vitality** | **High** | 40+ | Increases maximum HP to survive boss AOE attacks. |
| **Dexterity** | **Medium** | 20+ | Improves movement speed and casting animations. |
| **Strength** | **Low** | 10 | Only needed for meeting basic weapon requirements. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always prioritize reaching 15 Intelligence first. Without this, you cannot perform the basic healing sequence, rendering your support role ineffective in the early game.

## Essential Spells and Casting Sequences

Magic in Exoria is cast through specific keyboard sequences. As a support player, you must memorize these combinations to react quickly during intense battles. Unlike damage dealers who spam fireballs, a support player must time their "Bellow Ball" and healing spells to coincide with the party's needs.

To cast a spell, you must enter the letter sequence and then click to execute the magic. This requires both precision and timing, especially when a boss is targeting you.

## Optimizing Your Exoria Support Build Exoria Class Synergy

While Exoria offers five main classes—Spearman, Hunt, Fist, Hammer, Dagger, and Sword—not all are created equal when it comes to supporting. For a support build, you want a class that offers either range or defensive utility.

1.  **Spearman:** Excellent for support due to its long reach. This allows you to poke enemies and build "Souls" while staying safely behind your frontline tanks.
2.  **Sword:** A balanced choice that provides decent defensive options. It is the most reliable class for beginners transitioning into a support role.
3.  **Hammer:** While slow, the Hammer provides significant stagger. A support player using a Hammer can "stun lock" NPCs, giving the team time to recover.

## Progression and Soul Farming

To unlock the full potential of your **exoria support build exoria**, you will need to farm Souls. Souls are the currency used to learn advanced ability skills for your chosen class. Most high-level support skills require approximately **1,500 Souls** per skill.

The most efficient way to farm these is by completing the quest system located in the Guild Hall. You must first register for 15 Gold. Once registered, follow this progression path:

*   **Orcs:** Kill 15 Orcs to gain early XP and Souls.
*   **The Boss:** Participate in the boss dungeon near the castle. The boss's health scales with the number of players, so bring a team and focus on your healing rotations.
*   **Pirates:** Once you reach higher levels, farm Pirates and the Pirate Captain for the best soul-per-hour rates.

> **Warning:** Hogs may look like starter mobs, but they deal surprisingly high damage. Avoid farming them for Souls unless you have sufficient Vitality.

## Using Crystals to Boost Your Build

Throughout the map, you may find Intelligence and Dexterity crystals. These are rare drops from mobs or can be found as hidden trinkets. Eating these crystals grants a permanent +5 bonus to the respective stat. For a support build, hunting for Intelligence crystals is the fastest way to reach your healing milestones without spending all your level-up points.

1.  **Search the Docks:** Check behind crates and near the water for hidden trinkets.
2.  **Farm Human NPCs:** They have a higher chance of dropping stat-boosting crystals compared to animals.
3.  **Max Your Stats:** There is a limit to how many crystals you can consume per stat, so keep track of your "Tab" menu to see when you have reached the cap.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most important stat for an exoria support build exoria?**

A: Intelligence is the most important stat. You need at least 15 Intelligence to unlock the healing spell, and higher amounts will increase the total health restored to your teammates.

**Q: How do I cast the healing spell in Exoria?**

A: You must type the sequence **B-E-R-B** on your keyboard and then click your mouse to activate the heal. Make sure you have enough mana and the required Intelligence stat before attempting.

**Q: Can I play a support build solo?**

A: Yes, but it is slower than a pure Strength or Dexterity build. You should use the Fireball spell (E-R-C) to deal damage while using your healing spells to sustain yourself during fights with Orcs and Pirates.

**Q: Where do I get the 15 Gold needed to start quests?**

A: You can earn Gold by picking up trinkets found around the map or by killing weak human NPCs near the starting castle. Avoid the Hogs early on, as they are often too strong for level 1 players.
