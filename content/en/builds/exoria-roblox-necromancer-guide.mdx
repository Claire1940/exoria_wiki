## Core Abilities: Turning Enemies into Allies

The defining feature of the Necromancer is its ability to convert fallen foes into loyal soldiers. This passive mechanic ensures that the longer a battle lasts, the stronger your presence becomes on the battlefield. Understanding the nuances of this conversion is key to following any **exoria roblox necromancer guide** successfully.

### Passive: Soul Conversion
Every time you defeat a non-boss enemy, there is a **15% chance** to automatically convert it into an ally. These converted zombies are not just mindless drones; they retain the original strength and special attributes of the enemy type they were before death.

*   **Duration:** Converted allies stay active for approximately **2 minutes** or until they are defeated by enemies.
*   **Scaling:** The stronger the enemy you kill, the more powerful your ally will be.
*   **Automation:** Allies automatically seek out and attack the nearest hostile targets, allowing you to focus on movement and positioning.

### Active Skill: Call of the Dead
While the passive conversion provides a steady stream of reinforcements, "Call of the Dead" is your "panic button" and primary offensive tool. This skill allows you to manually intervene in the flow of combat.

| Skill Feature | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Max Revives** | Up to **10 nearby dead zombies** |
| **Cooldown** | **2 minutes** |
| **Ally Retention** | Keeps original shields, armor, and damage stats |
| **Strategic Use** | Best used near piles of high-tier elite corpses |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not waste Call of the Dead on basic walkers. Wait until you have defeated a group of armored or high-damage enemies to maximize the strength of your revived squad.

## Mastering the Exoria Roblox Necromancer Guide Tactics

To truly excel, you must change how you view the enemy. In most classes, a swarm of high-health enemies is a threat. For a Necromancer, it is an opportunity to recruit a high-tier frontline. Following these tactical steps will help you maintain a permanent army during long survival runs.

### 1. High-Priority Targeting
Since your allies retain their original stats, you should prioritize killing enemies that have utility. For example, if you see an enemy with a shield, focus your fire on them. Once they are converted or revived via Call of the Dead, that shield will now protect you and your teammates from incoming projectiles.

### 2. Positioning and Proximity
The Necromancer is most effective when staying near the front lines, but not directly in the line of fire. You need to be close enough to defeated enemies for the "Call of the Dead" range to trigger, but mobile enough to avoid being cornered. 

### 3. Wave Management
During early waves, the 15% conversion rate might feel slow. However, as the enemy density increases in later waves, the law of large numbers takes over. By wave 20 and beyond, you should have a near-constant army of 5-10 zombies assisting you at all times.

## Advanced Progression and Synergy

In the 2026 meta of Exoria, the Necromancer shines brightest when paired with support classes. While you provide the meat shields, classes like the Medic can actually keep your converted zombies alive longer, extending their 2-minute lifespan by preventing them from being burst down by bosses.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Necromancer worth 1,000 gems in 2026?**

A: Yes, the Necromancer is widely considered one of the best late-game classes in the **exoria roblox necromancer guide** due to its ability to scale with enemy difficulty. The stronger the enemies become, the stronger your army becomes.

**Q: Can I revive more than 10 zombies at once?**

A: No, the Call of the Dead ability is capped at 10 zombies per cast. However, these 10 zombies are in addition to any allies you have gained through the 15% passive conversion chance.

**Q: Do my allies follow me between different areas of the map?**

A: Yes, converted allies will follow you as long as they are within their 2-minute lifespan. If you move too far away, they will attempt to pathfind toward your current location while attacking enemies along the way.

**Q: What happens if I use Call of the Dead when there are no dead bodies nearby?**

A: The ability will go on cooldown, but no allies will be summoned. It is critical to ensure you are standing near a pile of defeated non-boss enemies before activating the skill.
