### Combat and Spellcasting

Magic in Exoria is categorized by its input method. While some players assume they need a physical orb to cast spells, advanced players know that certain "scale styles" allow for casting without any held items. This is particularly useful for hybrid builds that want to use a sword while maintaining the ability to cast fire or healing spells.

1.  **Healing Spells:** Activated by specific number sequences. It is essential to keep your mana pool high, as healing is the only way to survive the high-damage output of dungeon bosses.
2.  **Offensive Magic:** Fire spells are the most common starting point. Aiming is manual, meaning you must lead your targets to land a hit.
3.  **Physical Combat:** Basic swords can be purchased for around 25 gold. While they seem "light work" to acquire, upgrading them is where the real grind begins.

## Navigating Dungeons and Bosses

The dungeons in Exoria, such as the "duck hole," are designed to punish players who rush in without a plan. These areas often feature bosses with "balls on top of them"—unique visual indicators for their power levels or phase shifts.

### Essential Dungeon Checklist

*   **Check the Quest Board:** Before heading out, always visit the daily quest board outside the main town buildings. Completing these provides the gold necessary for gear upgrades.
*   **Party Up:** Entering a dungeon solo is a recipe for disaster. Use the **exoria discord** to find at least two other players.
*   **Inventory Management:** Ensure you have enough crystals. Crystals are often used for life-saves or as a resource for high-level spells.
*   **Identify the Boss Mechanics:** Some bosses put up "brick walls" or barriers. You must time your heavy attacks to break these before the boss can regenerate health.

> **Warning:** Falling short in a dungeon jump-puzzle can lead to instant death. Precision movement is just as important as high DPS.
