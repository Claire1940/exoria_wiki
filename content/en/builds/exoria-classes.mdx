## Racial Forms: Non-Demon vs. Demon

One of the most unique aspects of the **exoria classes** system is how your chosen race alters your appearance and mechanical ceiling. The game distinguishes heavily between Non-Demon and Demon races, particularly when it comes to transformations.

Non-demon races (such as Humans or Celestials) focus on "Purity" forms. These transformations often emphasize speed, clarity, and sustained damage. On the other hand, Demon races tap into "Chaos" forms, which provide massive burst damage and crowd control at the cost of higher stamina consumption or health drain mechanics.

### Form Comparison Matrix

| Feature | Non-Demon Forms | Demon Forms |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Style** | Radiant/Aura based | Dark/Ethereal based |
| **Transformation Cut** | Focused & Brief | Cinematic & Aggressive |
| **Stat Bonus** | **+15% Agility** | **+20% Strength** |
| **Sustainability** | High (Low drain) | Low (High drain) |

Each form comes with its own unique transformation cutscene. These are not merely cosmetic; they often provide a brief moment of invulnerability (i-frames) that can be used strategically to dodge a lethal blow while transitioning into a more powerful state.

## Mastering Transformation Mechanics

In Exoria, knowing when to transform is just as important as knowing which skills to use. Since each of the **exoria classes** has a unique interaction with its racial form, players must monitor their "Transformation Meter" closely.

1.  **Build Meter:** Land consistent hits with base sword skills to fill the gauge.
2.  **Timing:** Activate the transformation during a boss's transition phase or when an opponent is low on stamina.
3.  **Skill Chaining:** Use the enhanced versions of your skills immediately, as the transformation duration is limited in the 2026 meta.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Being interrupted during the transformation cutscene can sometimes result in a wasted meter without gaining the full buff. Ensure you have a small window of safety before activating.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch between exoria classes after I have started?**

A: Yes, but it requires a Class Reset token or reaching a specific milestone in the main questline. It is generally better to focus on one class per character to maximize your skill points.

**Q: Are the upgraded sword skills available to all races?**

A: Yes, the upgraded sword skills are available to everyone. However, the *visuals* and *secondary effects* of those skills will change depending on whether you are a Demon or a Non-Demon race.

**Q: How do I unlock the unique transformation cutscenes?**

A: Transformation cuts are automatically unlocked once you reach the required mastery level for your specific race and class combination. You can toggle the length of these animations in the settings menu.

**Q: Which race is best for the Sword Class in 2026?**

A: For pure DPS, Demon races are currently leading the meta. However, for players who prefer a more forgiving playstyle with better defensive options, Non-Demon races offer superior consistency in high-level raids.
