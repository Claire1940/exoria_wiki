## Analyzing the Exoria Roblox Best Class for Your Playstyle

When determining the **exoria roblox best class**, you must categorize them by their role in combat. The game currently offers a mix of melee bruisers, high-mobility assassins, and magical glass cannons.

### The Brute: The Budget King
The Brute is often overlooked because of its low entry cost, but in 2026, it remains the most efficient starter class. With a total cost of only 3,750 souls to max out, it allows players to reach their power spike much faster than any other path. This class focuses on high health pools and physical resistance, making it ideal for those who prefer to dive headfirst into enemy mobs without worrying about complex skill rotations.

### The Rogue: High Investment, High Reward
If you are looking for the absolute highest ceiling for damage, the Rogue is frequently cited as the **exoria roblox best class** for veteran players. However, this power comes at a steep price. With five distinct skills costing 2,500 souls each, you will need a total of 12,500 souls to see the Rogue's true potential. Its playstyle revolves around stealth, critical hits, and rapid movement, which requires a high level of mechanical skill to master.

### The Mage and Monk: The Versatile Casters
Both the Mage and Monk require a flat 10,000 souls to reach maximum proficiency. The Mage serves as the primary ranged damage dealer, utilizing elemental spells to control the battlefield from a distance. The Monk, conversely, offers a hybrid approach, blending physical strikes with spiritual buffs. These classes are excellent for players who enjoy a more tactical approach to combat.

## Farming Souls for Your Class

Since souls are the gatekeeper to your power, efficiency in farming is key. 
*   **Target Elites:** Higher-level enemies drop significantly more souls per kill than standard mobs.

*   **Group Farming:** Being in a party allows you to clear waves faster, leading to a higher "Souls Per Hour" (SPH) rate.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the cheapest class to max out in Exoria?**

A: The Brute is currently the most affordable class to max out, requiring only 1,250 souls per skill for a total of 3,750 souls. This makes it an excellent choice for players who want to reach their full potential quickly without a massive grind.

**Q: Which class is considered the exoria roblox best class for solo players?**

A: The Monk and the Brute are often considered the best for solo play. The Brute offers high survivability through raw health, while the Monk provides a balance of damage and self-sustain that allows players to tackle difficult content without a dedicated healer.

**Q: Why does the Rogue cost more souls than the other classes?**

A: The Rogue features more skills to unlock than classes like the Fighter or Brute. While most classes have 3 or 4 skills, the Rogue has 5 skills at 2,500 souls each, totaling 12,500. This higher cost reflects its complex high-damage ceiling and utility.

**Q: Can I change my class after spending souls on skills?**

A: You can change your class by visiting a different trainer, but be aware that your progress in your current class is typically not refunded. It is highly recommended to save enough souls to buy at least two skills in your new class before making the switch.
