## The Evolution of the Sword Class

The sword path has long been a staple of the Exoria experience, offering a balanced mix of mobility, defense, and high-burst damage. However, the 2026 update has overhauled the base class into something far more dynamic. Players no longer simply swing a blade; they must now manage "Flow" and "Stance" mechanics that reward precision timing and combo execution.

The upgraded version of the base sword class is not just a statistical increase in damage. It introduces entirely new animations and hitboxes that require players to relearn their spacing. The transition to the upgraded form occurs after completing the "Blades of the Ancestors" questline, which tests your proficiency with the standard kit before granting access to the advanced techniques.

## Race-Specific Transformations: Demon vs. Non-Demon

One of the most significant changes to the **exoria class** structure is the implementation of race-locked transformations. Your character's race now dictates the visual and mechanical "flavor" of your ultimate form. These transformations are not merely cosmetic; they alter your skill properties and provide massive passive buffs.

### Non-Demon Forms
Non-demon races, such as Humans and Celestials, focus on "Purity" and "Technique." Their transformations typically involve glowing auras and streamlined sword movements. These forms are designed for players who prefer sustained DPS (Damage Per Second) and high utility. The non-demon sword class upgrades emphasize speed and cooldown reduction, allowing for a relentless flurry of attacks.

### Demon Forms
Conversely, demon races tap into "Chaos" and "Power." Their transformation cutscenes are often more aggressive, featuring dark energy and visceral weapon shifts. The demon version of the sword class focuses on "Lifesteal" and "Burst Damage." While they may strike slower than their non-demon counterparts, each hit carries significantly more weight and can often break through enemy guards with ease.

## Mastering the New Sword Skills

The upgraded skills for the **exoria class** require more than just mashing buttons. Each skill now has a "Perfect Timing" window. If you activate a follow-up skill exactly when your character's blade flashes, you trigger a "Resonance" effect. This increases the damage of the next skill by 20% and reduces its mana cost.

### Advanced Combo Strings
To maximize your output, you must integrate your racial transformation into your combo rotation. A common mistake is using the transformation as a panic button. Instead, it should be used as a combo extender.

1.  **Initiate** with Skyward Thrust to launch the target.
2.  **Follow up** with a mid-air Lunar Slash (Upgraded).
3.  **Activate Transformation** mid-air to reset your dash cooldown.
4.  **Execute** Vanguard Dash to slam the target back to the ground.
5.  **Finish** with the Ultimate Skill while the target is grounded.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Transformations have a long internal cooldown. If you waste your form during a minor mob encounter, you may find yourself defenseless when a high-level player or mini-boss appears.

## Optimizing Your Build for 2026

To truly excel with the **exoria class**, your equipment must complement your upgraded skills. For sword users, the focus should be on "Physical Penetration" and "Attack Speed." Even the most powerful demon form will struggle if its attacks are too slow to connect with agile opponents.

For non-demon forms, look for gear that grants "Cooldown Recovery Rate." Since your strength lies in skill cycling, being able to use your Vanguard Dash more frequently is your best defensive and offensive tool. For demon forms, "Crit Chance" is the priority, as it triggers your innate lifesteal more often, making you nearly unkillable in 1v1 duels.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch between Demon and Non-Demon forms for my exoria class?**

A: No, your form is determined by your character's race chosen at the start of the game. If you wish to experience the other transformation type, you will need to use a Race Change orb or create a new character.

**Q: Is the upgraded sword class better than the magic class in 2026?**

A: Both classes are balanced for different roles. The sword class offers better survivability and consistent damage, while the magic class provides superior area-of-effect (AoE) damage and crowd control.

**Q: How do I trigger the unique transformation cutscene?**

A: The cutscene triggers automatically the first time you activate your transformation after completing the "Blades of the Ancestors" quest. You can re-watch these cutscenes in the "Memories" tab of your character menu.

**Q: Do the upgraded skills consume more Mana?**

A: Yes, upgraded skills generally have a higher Mana cost. It is recommended to invest points into your Intelligence or Spirit stats, or use Mana-regen accessories to sustain your combos during long fights.
