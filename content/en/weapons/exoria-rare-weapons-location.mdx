## Starting Your Journey: The Basic Spear Trainer

Before hunting for legendary gear, every player needs a solid foundation. The basic weapon trainer is located near the main castle. To find him, exit the castle, take a right, and head straight until you see a fallen tree branch. You can either use the ladder nearby or scale the mountain directly to reach the NPC.

| Item/Service | Cost | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Spear** | 30 Gold | None |
| **Skill Unlocks** | ~1,500 Souls | Level 1+ |

> **💡 Tip:** While the game states skills cost 1,250 to 1,500 souls, players often encounter a bug where the NPC refuses the trade. It is highly recommended to farm at least **2,000 souls** before attempting to learn your first class skills to ensure the transaction processes correctly.

## Mid-Tier Gear: The Coral Weapons

Once you have outgrown your basic equipment, your next stop should be the "Water Houses" area. This location is often confused with the starting docks, but it is a distinct zone featuring buildings constructed directly over the water. This is the primary **exoria rare weapons location** for mid-game players who need a boost in stats without venturing into the high-difficulty dungeons.

The Coral set is known for its distinctive blue aesthetic and improved reach. You can find the following items here:

*   **Coral Spear:** 65 Gold
*   **Coral Sword:** Approximately 60-70 Gold
*   **Coral Dagger:** Approximately 55 Gold

To reach this area, head toward the docks from the spawn point and look for the cluster of houses elevated above the waves. The vendor stands on the wooden walkways connecting the structures.

## Finding the Rare Dungeon Weapons

The absolute best gear in the game is hidden within the Dungeon. This **exoria rare weapons location** is significantly more dangerous than the previous zones, as it is guarded by high-level zombies and environmental hazards.

### How to Reach the Dungeon
1.  Locate the **second exit** of the Kingdom.
2.  Look for the massive stone tower in the distance; this is your destination.
3.  Take a right from the exit and follow the path toward the tower.
4.  Navigate the climbing puzzles and ladders to reach the entrance.

### Navigating the Dungeon Maze
The Dungeon can be incredibly dark, making navigation nearly impossible without a light source. Use your **Velo Ball** magic (typically mapped to the 'E' or 'R' key) to illuminate the corridors. The most efficient way to reach the weapon room is to **take every possible left turn**. Following this "all-lefts" strategy will lead you directly past the boss room entrance and into the secret armory.

### Rare Dungeon Weapon Costs
Once you reach the armory at the end of the left-turn path, you will find three of the most powerful weapons currently in Exoria.

| Weapon Name | Type | Cost (Gold) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Spear** | Spear | 150 |
| **Dark Heart** | Dagger | 140 |

These weapons represent the current "Best in Slot" for their respective classes. The Dark Heart dagger is particularly prized for its high attack speed, while the Shadows sword offers the best raw damage per swing for melee players.

## Mastering Spear Combat Mechanics

If you choose to use the spears found at these locations, you must master the unique move set to be effective in PVP and PVE. The Spear class relies heavily on ragdolling enemies to prevent them from counter-attacking.

*   **Rapid Attack (Shift + M1):** A flurry of stabs useful for breaking shields.
*   **Impale (Shift + M2):** A powerful thrust that causes a ragdoll effect.
*   **Aerial Strike (4x M1 + M2):** A combo that launches the enemy into the air. You can follow this up with a mid-air M2 for a "slam" finish.
*   **Front Flip (Shift + Space):** A high-damage acrobatic move. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Front Flip (Shift + Space) has a long wind-up animation. Do not use this move if an enemy is already hitting you, as their attacks will cancel your animation and put the skill on cooldown without dealing damage. Use it as an opener or while the enemy is recovering from a ragdoll.

## Summary of Weapon Progression
For players looking to optimize their route, follow this progression path to ensure you are always properly geared for the challenges ahead:

1.  **Early Game:** Farm 30 Gold and 1,500 Souls at the Castle area for the Basic Spear and skills.
2.  **Mid Game:** Travel to the Water Houses/Docks for the Coral Spear (65 Gold).
3.  **Endgame:** Enter the Dungeon Tower, use the "Left-Turn Only" method, and purchase the Shadows Sword or Dark Heart for 140-150 Gold.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best exoria rare weapons location for daggers?**

A: The best dagger in the game, the Dark Heart, is located in the Dungeon's secret armory. It costs 140 Gold and can be reached by taking every left turn inside the Dungeon maze.

**Q: How many souls do I actually need for spear skills?**

A: While the trainer says 1,250 or 1,500, many players find they need closer to 2,000 souls for the game to register the purchase. It is best to over-farm slightly to avoid making multiple trips to the trainer.

**Q: Is the Coral Spear better than the Basic Spear?**

A: Yes. The Coral Spear, found at the Water Houses, costs 65 Gold and offers better range and damage stats than the 30 Gold starter version.

**Q: Can Mages use these rare weapons?**

A: Currently, the Mage class does not utilize physical weapons in the same way as the Spear, Sword, or Dagger classes. Mages rely on their magic abilities, though they may still need to visit specific NPCs to upgrade their casting power.
