## S-Tier: The Endgame Powerhouses

The S-Tier is occupied exclusively by Tier 3 weapons found in the Castellan region. These weapons offer the highest scaling with your primary stats and are necessary for clearing endgame boss dungeons.

### Castellan Hammer (Tier 3)
The Castellan Hammer is the undisputed king of raw power. It features the highest damage-per-hit in the game, making it ideal for players who invest heavily into the Strength stat. When paired with Tier 3 Hammer Armor, the damage mitigation and impact force become nearly unstoppable.

### Castellan Spear (Tier 3)
Many veteran players consider the Spear to be the most efficient choice on the **exoria best weapon tier list**. While its individual hits do less damage than the Hammer, its incredible range allows you to "kite" enemies, hitting them before they can enter their own attack animation. In a game where losing a life is a permanent setback, the safety provided by the Spear's reach is invaluable.

## A-Tier: Reliable Mid-Game Options

A-Tier weapons are primarily the "Coral" series (Tier 2). These are significant upgrades over the starting equipment and are required to survive the transition from the Goblin Camps to the Sky Castle.

*   **Coral Spear:** Offers a significant damage bump over the T1 version while maintaining the signature reach.
*   **Coral Sword:** The most versatile weapon for players who haven't committed to a specialized Strength or Dexterity build yet.
*   **Castellan Dagger (T3):** While a Tier 3 weapon, it sits in A-Tier because its short range requires immense skill to use against bosses without taking damage.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to skip Tier 2 weapons if you are playing solo. The jump in enemy health from the Goblin Camp to the Sky Castle is too steep for Tier 1 gear.

## Understanding Stat Scaling for Your Build

Your position on the **exoria best weapon tier list** is heavily influenced by how you allocate your stat points. A Tier 3 Hammer is useless if you have invested all your points into Dexterity and Intelligence.

### Vitality and Strength
For Hammer and Sword users, Strength is the priority. It directly increases your damage output. However, since these weapons require you to be in close proximity to enemies, you must supplement this with Vitality to increase your maximum health.

### Dexterity and Faith
Spear and Dagger users benefit most from Dexterity, which governs your maximum stamina. This allows for more frequent dodges and longer combos. Faith is a niche stat often used by support-oriented players to increase healing output, though it has less direct impact on weapon damage in the current 2026 build.

### Intelligence
Intelligence increases your Mana pool. This is vital if you have unlocked magic spells like Fireball or Heal. Even melee-focused players should consider a small investment in Intelligence to ensure they can cast utility spells during difficult boss encounters.

## How to Obtain Tier 3 Weapons

To acquire the top-rated items on the **exoria best weapon tier list**, you must reach the Sky Castle. This involves navigating to the large wooden structure and using the elevator platform.

1.  **Preparation:** Ensure you are at least level 40 and have a full squad.
2.  **The Elevator:** Locate the lever at the wooden structure to bring the platform down. Be careful not to stand directly under it.
3.  **The Dungeon:** Once at the top, navigate past the high-level mobs.
4.  **Secret Locations:** The Castellan Hammer and its corresponding armor are hidden in a lower basement area reached via stairs. The Spear, Sword, and Dagger are grouped together in a separate wing of the castle.

> **💡 Tip:** Use "Magic C" or a light-source trinket when exploring the Sky Castle, as the visibility is extremely low and falling off the edges results in an instant loss of a life slot.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the absolute best weapon in the exoria best weapon tier list for solo players?**

A: The **Castellan Spear (Tier 3)** is generally considered the best for solo play. Its superior range allows you to hit enemies without taking damage, which is crucial for preserving your limited life slots when you don't have a healer.

**Q: How do I unlock the Monk/Fist fighting style?**

A: You must find the Monk Trainer located near the Goblin Camp. Training costs 2,500 Souls. This style is highly effective for players who prefer high-speed combos and don't want to rely on physical weapon drops.

**Q: Can I upgrade my Tier 1 weapons into Tier 2?**

A: No, weapons cannot be upgraded between tiers. You must purchase the new tier of weapons from the appropriate blacksmith or find them in world chests. However, you can use crystals dropped by bosses to boost the specific stats that your weapons scale with.

**Q: Where can I get more Gold Crowns for high-tier weapons?**

A: The fastest way to earn Gold is by completing quests from the Copper Hunter NPC (requires a 15 Gold initiation fee) or by finding and selling trinkets hidden throughout the map. Dungeons also provide a high yield of both Gold and Souls.
