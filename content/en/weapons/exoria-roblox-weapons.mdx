## Weapon Tiers and Basic Locations

Weapons in Exoria are currently split into three primary tiers. Tier 1 weapons are easily accessible within the starting town, while Tier 2 and Tier 3 require venturing into dangerous territories like the Goblin Camp and the Sky Castle.

| Tier | Weapon Name | Primary Location | Access Requirements |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | Basic Sword/Dagger | Fighting Chance Shop | 50+ Gold |
| **Tier 1** | Hammer | Guild Hall | 50+ Gold |
| **Tier 1** | Spear | Spear Trainer (Golems) | 50+ Gold |
| **Tier 2** | Coral Weapons | Goblin Camp Blacksmith | Mid-Level Survival |
| **Tier 3** | Castellan Weapons | Sky Castle / Dungeon | High Level / Elevator Access |

### Tier 1 Weapons: The Starter Set
For new players, your first stop should be the "Fighting Chance" shop located in the main town. Here, you can purchase the basic Sword and Dagger. The Dagger is located in a showcase closer to the entrance, while the Sword is situated further back near the NPC.

The Hammer is found in the Guild Hall, conveniently placed right next to the Hammer Trainer. The Spear, often considered a fan favorite due to its superior range and speed, is the most difficult Tier 1 item to find. You must travel to the Golem area, climb a wooden ladder, and speak to the Spear Trainer to purchase this weapon.

## Tier 2 and Tier 3 Advanced Gear

Once you have outgrown your basic equipment, you must venture further out to upgrade your **exoria roblox weapons** to the Coral or Castellan variants.

### Finding Tier 2 Coral Weapons
All Tier 2 weapons are consolidated in a single location: the Goblin Camp. To reach this area, follow the path toward the Fist Trainer but continue straight instead of ascending the mountain. You will encounter a small city within the camp where a local blacksmith sells the Tier 2 Hammer, Dagger, Sword, and Spear.

### Reaching Tier 3 Castellan Weapons
Tier 3 gear is hidden within the Sky Castle dungeon. Accessing this area requires finding a large wooden structure with an elevator. 
1. Activate the lever to bring the platform down.
2. Ride the elevator to the Sky Castle.
3. Follow the stairs down to a secret location known as Castellan.
4. The Hammer and its corresponding armor are located in the lower basement.
5. The Tier 3 Spear, Sword, and Dagger are grouped together in a separate chamber past the main dungeon corridor.

## Crafting and Blacksmithing Guide

While finding weapons in shops is straightforward, the most powerful mid-game utility items and weapons are often crafted. Blacksmithing is a lucrative life skill that allows you to create Shadow Steel gear, which sells for a high price and offers excellent stats.

### Essential Life Skill Synergy
To succeed in blacksmithing, you must also level your Woodcutting. Many high-tier weapons require Pine Handles or Pine Poles.

*   **Woodcutting Level 10:** Unlocks the ability to craft basic poles and handles.
*   **Blacksmithing Level 15:** Unlocks Shadow Steel weapon crafting.
*   **Mining:** Required to harvest Shadow Steel ore from the caves past the graveyard.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When crafting, ensure your inventory weight is at 31/32 or lower. If your inventory is completely full (32/32), the game may consume your resources without granting the finished item or XP.

### Shadow Steel Material Locations
To craft Shadow Steel tools and weapons, you need to head to the "Past Recollections" area. This zone is located past the graveyard.
*   **Shadow Steel Ore:** Found inside a cave inhabited by shadow people. The ore respawns quickly, so you can cycle between three nodes near the entrance.
*   **Red Cedar:** These large red trees are located just outside the Shadow Steel cave. They require a higher woodcutting level (Level 20) to process effectively.

| Item | Required Materials | Blacksmith Level |
|---|---|---|
| **Shadow Steel Ax** | 3 Logs + 6 Shadow Steel | 15 |
| **Shadow Steel Pickaxe** | 3 Logs + 6 Shadow Steel | 15 |
| **Heavy Gauntlet** | 2 Shadow Steel | 15 |
| **Shadow Steel Sword** | Pine Pole + Shadow Steel | 20 |

## Life Skill Leveling Milestones

Progressing through the ranks of Alchemy, Blacksmithing, and Cooking provides titles and unlocks new recipes for your **exoria roblox weapons** and utility items.

1.  **Novice:** Level 10
2.  **Adept:** Level 25
3.  **Expert:** Level 50
4.  **Master:** Level 75
5.  **World:** Level 100

For those looking to make money quickly, spamming the creation of Wooden Poles (which cost two logs) is the fastest way to reach Woodcutting Level 10. Once you unlock Pine, you can harvest wood much faster, as Pine trees typically only require two to four hits to fell.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best weapon for beginners in Exoria?**

A: The Spear is widely considered the best starter weapon. While its raw damage is comparable to the sword, its superior reach and fast attack speed allow you to hit enemies from a safe distance, which is vital for early-game survival.

**Q: Where can I find the Shadow Steel blacksmith?**

A: You do not need a specific NPC to craft Shadow Steel. Once you have the materials, head to the Forge station in the main town. As long as your Blacksmithing level is 15 or higher, you can select the Shadow Steel recipes from the crafting menu.

**Q: Is the Shadow Steel Ax bugged?**

A: As of the 2026 update, there is a known visual bug where crafting a Shadow Steel Ax might display a different model in the crafting window. However, the stats remain consistent with Shadow Steel tier gear, so it is still worth crafting for its efficiency.

**Q: How do I get to the Tundra for cooking?**

A: To access the Tundra and the kitchen area, you must pass through the Field Hive Gates. This area is essential for leveling your cooking skill, though currently, Raw Pork is the only consistent ingredient available for high-level leveling.

**Q: Can I skip Tier 2 and go straight to Tier 3 weapons?**

A: While it is possible to enter the Sky Castle at a low level, the enemies there are extremely powerful and can often one-shot unprepared players. It is highly recommended to at least have Tier 2 Coral gear or Shadow Steel equipment before attempting the Tier 3 dungeon.
