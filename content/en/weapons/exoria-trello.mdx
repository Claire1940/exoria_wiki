## Understanding the Core Systems

Before venturing into the wild, every player must understand the three pillars of survival in Exoria: Lives, Currency, and Souls. Unlike many other RPGs, death in Exoria carries a heavy price. You begin with three hearts, representing your total lives. If you lose all three, your character is wiped, and you must start your journey from scratch.

| Resource | Purpose | How to Obtain |
|---|---|---|
| **Gold** | Used to purchase weapons, armor, and basic supplies. | Looting chests, selling trinkets, and killing mobs. |
| **Souls** | Required to purchase class-specific skills from trainers. | Dropped by enemies upon death (Orcs, Pirates, Bosses). |
| **Crystals** | Provides permanent small increases to base stats. | Rare drops from basic mobs like Orcs. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always keep an eye on your heart count. If you are down to your last life, prioritize safety and avoid bosses until you can recover or gear up significantly.

## Unlocking Magic and Early Progression

Your first priority after spawning at the docks is to unlock your magic. Magic is not just for offense; it provides the "Self-Heal" ability, which is the single most important skill for early-game survival. To unlock your magic, head to the Orc territory located just outside the main town. You do not need a weapon yet—simply punching the Orcs approximately ten times will trigger your magical awakening.

Once unlocked, you can perform spells using specific key combinations. These spells are essential for maintaining your health while grinding for Gold and Souls.

| Spell Name | Key Combination | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Self-Heal** | V + E + R + V + Click | Restores a portion of your health instantly. |
| **Light Orb** | C + E + R + Click | Creates a floating light source for dark areas. |

After securing your magic, focus on farming Orcs until you have at least 75 to 100 Gold. During this process, Orcs may drop stat crystals. Use these immediately, as they provide permanent buffs that persist even if you haven't specialized in a class yet.

## Navigating the Exoria Trello for Equipment

As you move toward the mid-game, the **exoria trello** becomes your most valuable tool. The **exoria trello** lists every piece of equipment currently available in the game, along with their specific stat requirements and drop locations. Because the game does not always provide clear directions on where to find high-tier loot, cross-referencing your current level with the Trello's armor list is the most efficient way to progress.

For beginners, the first major equipment goal should be the **Ghoul Armor**. This set provides a massive defensive boost for only 20 Gold, but finding it requires navigating the dark Crypts.

### How to Get the Ghoul Armor
1. **Prepare your Light Orb:** Use the C-E-R-Click combination before entering the Crypt, as it is pitch black.
2. **Find the Elevator:** Located to the right of the main town gate. Pull the lever to ascend.
3. **The Secret Hole:** Once inside the Crypt, look for a small hole in the wall near the back. 
4. **Spam Roll:** You cannot walk through the gap. You must use the basic "Roll" mechanic repeatedly to glitch through the opening.
5. **Purchase:** The Ghoul Armor stands inside this hidden room. It is a one-time purchase that will carry you through the mid-game.

## Class Selection and Soul Farming

Once you have your armor and basic magic, it is time to choose a class. There are currently five primary paths: Hammer, Spear, Dagger, Monk, and Sword. While the **exoria trello** provides the exact stats for these classes, community consensus currently ranks **Monk** and **Spear** as the most beginner-friendly due to their high damage output and reach.

To fully master a class, you will need between 7,000 and 10,000 Souls. This allows you to buy every skill from your class trainer immediately.

| Class | Primary Stat | Playstyle |
|---|---|---|
| **Monk** | Strength/Agility | Fast-paced melee with high combo potential. |
| **Spear** | Strength | Long-range melee, excellent for keeping mobs at a distance. |
| **Sword** | Strength | Balanced offense and defense; standard RPG experience. |
| **Dagger** | Agility | High critical hit rate but requires close proximity. |
| **Hammer** | Strength | Slow, heavy hits that can stun enemies. |

To find your trainer, you can consult the [official Roblox community](https://www.roblox.com) or the community-run Discord. Most trainers are hidden in the wilderness, far from the safety of the town docks.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not purchase the "Dash" trainer too early. The basic "Roll" mechanic is often more reliable for escaping enemy combos because it has a more predictable hitbox and lower cooldown.

## Advanced Survival Strategies

As you approach the endgame content available in 2026, combat becomes significantly more dangerous. Mobs will begin to combo you, which can lead to a quick loss of life if you aren't careful.

### Escaping Combos
If you find yourself trapped by multiple enemies, do not try to out-run them. Instead, use your **Roll** to break the stun-lock. Rolling provides a brief window of invulnerability that allows you to reposition and cast your Self-Heal spell.

### Efficient Gold Farming
While killing mobs provides XP and Souls, it is a slow way to earn Gold. The most efficient method for wealth accumulation is gathering trinkets scattered across the map. These items can be sold at the town vendor for high prices without the risk of combat. This allows you to save up for high-tier weapons found on the **exoria trello** without risking your character's life.

### Boss Etiquette
Never attempt a boss alone during your first encounter. Bosses in Exoria scale their health based on the number of players in the party. While soloing a boss gives significantly more XP, the risk of a wipe is too high for lower-level players. Join a party, learn the boss's attack patterns, and only attempt a solo run once your gear exceeds the recommended stats listed on the Trello.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I access the Exoria Trello?**

A: The link to the **exoria trello** is typically found in the official Discord server's FAQ section or the game's description page. It is updated frequently by the developers to reflect balance changes and new item additions.

**Q: What happens if I lose all three lives?**

A: Your character, including all levels, souls, and items, will be wiped. You will have to start over from level one as a novice. Some permanent game passes may persist, but your in-game progress is reset.

**Q: Which race is the best in Exoria?**

A: Race is determined by your hair and skin color upon spawning. Each race has minor stat variations. You can check the "Race" tab on the Trello to see which buffs apply to your specific character appearance.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm Souls?**

A: For early levels, Orcs are the safest. As you reach level 10-15, moving to the Pirate camps will yield more Souls per hour, though the enemies are much more aggressive and use ranged attacks.
